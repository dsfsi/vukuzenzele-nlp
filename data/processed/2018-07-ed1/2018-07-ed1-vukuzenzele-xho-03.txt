Ukudalela ulutsha lwase-KZN imisebenzi

Hlengiwe Ngobese

Abantu abatsha  abangaphezulu  kwama-20 aba phuma kwiphondo laKwa Zulu-Natal baza kuxhamla kumzi-mveliso wakwaMahindra owakhiwe ngema li ezizigidi ezingama-95 eerandi. Lo mzi-mveliso ukufutshane nesixeko saseDurban uza kuvelisa uhlo bo olutsha lweeveni ezina macango amane, i-double cab, ezibizwa ngokuba zi-Pik Up.
Abantu abatsha abangama-25 baKwaZulu-Natal bafumene uqeqesho kunye nomsebenzi ngokuvulwa kwalo mzi-mveliso mtsha wakwaMahindra eDurban.
Abasebenzi bafumana uqeqesho oluqhutywa  yinkampani yakwaMahi ndra Group isebenzisana no-AIH Logistics futhi baza kuthunyelwa e-India ukuze bafumane olunye uqeqesho xa kukho imfuneko.
Umsebenzi wokuvelisa iimoto kodwa osalingwayo sele uqalile kulo mzi-mve liso kwaye kucetywe uku velisa iimoto ezingama 2 000 ngonyaka, inani elo liza kunyuka ngokuha mba kwexesha libe zimoto ezingama-4 000 ngonyaka.
"Olu tyalo-mali luza kwenza amanye amashishini aluthembe uqoqosho lweli lizwe. Oku kubonisa ukuba iKwaZulu-Natal liphondo elityebileyo ngamathuba otyalo-mali. Lo mzi-mve-liso ukwangumzekelo  wamalinge enziwa yiDurban okuthoba amaxhala kwabo bamathidala ukuza kutyala imali kwesi sixeko,‚Äù utshilo uMphathiswa wePhondo wezoPhuhliso loQoqosho, ezoKhenketho kunye nezeNdalo, uSihle Zikalala ngethuba kuvu lwa lo mzi-mveliso ngoku sesikweni.Uthi imisebenzi yokuqala edalwe ngulo mzi-mveliso iza kwandisa inani labantu abanezakhono kweli pho-ndo.
Omnye wabasebenzi balo mzi-mveliso nguSthembiso Mthembu onesidanga sobunjineli, uthi uvuya kakhulu ngalo msebenzi.
"Ndihleli ekhaya unyaka wonke ndingafumani msebenzi. Lo msebenzi  undincedile kuba ngoku ndiyakwazi ukuzondla mna nekhaya lam. Nango na ndinesidanga sobunji neli kwezokukhanda  iinjini noomatshini aku-kho nto ingako bendiyazi ngokwenziwa kweemoto.
Olu qeqesho silufumeneyo lusincedile satsho sazithe-mba nathi ngomsebenzi wethu," utshilo.
IsiKhulu seMisebenzi yeHlabathi sakwaMahindra u-Arvind Matthew uthi le nkampani ineenjongo zokuthenga ama-40%  eentsimbi ze-Pik Up apha eMzantsi Afrika.
"Ukwenziwa kwe-Pik  Up kweli linyathelo  lokuqala kwizicwangciso zethu zexesha elide, zona eziquka ukwandisa izithuba zemisebenzi kwiphondo kunye namathuba okuthengwa kweentsimbi ne-zinye izixhobo zokwenza iimoto KwaZulu-Natal.
