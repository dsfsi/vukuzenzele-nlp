Bafundzi etikolweni tase makhaya batfola lusito 

More Matshediso

Litiko Lemfundvo  Lesisekelo licale lu hlelo lolutawuhlo mulisa bafundzi labanyenti emimmangweni yase makhaya leyayincishwe ematfuba ekufundza phambi lini.
Lomklamo Wabasiti  Ngemfundvo Yasemakhaya (i-REAP) luhlelo lweminya ka lemitsatfu lolutawucala kusetjentiswa nga-2018 kute kube nga-2021 njengeluhle lo lwekulinga, kantsi luhlose kuhlomulisa tikolo leti-188 eNingizimu Afrika ngaleso sikhatsi.  Lelitiko linelitsemba lekutsi loku kutawufaka sandla ku mazinga ekufundza neku fundzisa kuloluhlelo lwemfu ndvo lesisekelo.
Loluhlelo lusalingwa eti godzini letimbili kusinye sa letifundza letintsatfu letinga kasebenti kahle etindzaweni tasemakhaya. Tifaka ekhatsi Sigodzi sase-Alfred Nzo East ne-OR Tambo Coastal eMphu malanga Kapa, Sigodzi sa se-ILembe nesase-UMzinya thi KwaZulu-Natal, kanye neSigodzi saseSekhukhune nesaseMopani eLimpopo.
Umcondzisi Lomkhulu weKusetjentiswa Kwekha rikhulamu neKunotsiswa kwayo kuLitiko Lemfundvo Lesisekelo Seliki Tlhabane, u tsite lelitiko selimeme bantfu  labasha labalinganiselwa kulabange-750 lebaphase matikuletjeni kutsi Basite Ngetemfundvo eTikolweni Tasemakhaya.
“Baceceshiwe kancane lo kutabenta kutsi bakhone kusebenta lemisebenti la baniketwe yona. Batawu fakwa kuSigaba sekuCala nalesiseMkhatsini, lekufaka ekhatsi Libanga 1 kuya ku4,”asho achaza.
Labasiti Ngetemfundvo Yasemakhaya balindze leke kutsi basite ngetintfo letihlukene letiphatselene nekharikhulamu, ikakhu lukati kwenta kancono  kunyumeresi, ilitheresi ne makhono ekufundza.
“Lena ngulenye indlela yekuniketa lusha emandla etindzaweni letinemnotfo longahambi kahle ngeku tsi kutfutfukiswe emakhono nelwati lwemsebenti,” kusho Tlhabane.Utsite Labasiti Ngemfu ndvo Yasemakhaya balindle ke kutsi behlise lomtfwalo wabothishela etikolweni tasemakhaya ngekutsi basite ngemisebenti lephatselene nekharikhulamu njengeku sebentisana nabo ngemi sebenti yasekhaya, emace mbu lenta tibalo, emace mbu lafundzako, emace mbu emisebenti yebuciko, nemiklamo yetekulima.
Umphatsisikolo Ntombi kayise Mkhize wasesiko lweni semabanga laphansi i-Ozwathini Primary lese ndzaweni yaseNodwengu esigodzini sase-ILembe une litsemba lekutsi loluhlelo lutawusita bafundzi ngema khono ekubala nekufundza.
Sikolo sakhe sinebafundzi laba-387 kusukela kuLiba nga R kuya ku-7 lababhalise kulomnyaka.
“Bafundzi betfu labanyenti bavela emakhaya lange nabatali kantsi labanye ba nakekelwa bogogo. Abanabo bantfu labangabasita ngemi sebenti yesikolo yasekhaya noma babakhutsate kutsi bafundze, kantsi loku kwe hlisa lizinga labenta ngalo eklasini,” asho achaza. “Lenye inkinga kwekutsi bafundzi betfu labasema bangeni laphansi bafundzi swa yonkhe intfo nge siZulu kantsi uma ba ndlulela kumabanga lase mkhatsini kufanele bagu cuke bente tonkhe tifundvo ngesiNgisi, ngako-ke kuba yinkinga lenkulu kubo kutsi bagucuke.
Ngalesikhatsi sekulinga, sikale lomtselela nemphume lelo ngekutsi sente lucwani ngo nekuhlola kwaloluhle lo lwe-REAP . Sibuye safuna lusito lalabasitfupha laba neticu tasenyuvesi kodvwa basengakasebenti njenge Bachumanisi baloMklamo. Batawuba khona kusinye ngasinye sigodzi lesinge nelele. Labachumanisi balo mklamo batawubuye basite kutsi bente lucwaningo ne luhlolo lwalomklamo.
Kuleminyaka lemibili  lendlulile, sente umhlanga no wekucala ngeMfundvo yaseMakhaya kulomkhakha wetemfundvo lesisekelo.
Kulomhlangano, lucwaningo lwetemfundvo loluhlu kahlukene lukhombe ku lenkinga yekutsi kuletinye taletinselele letibuketane netikolo tasemakhaya tikha la temsebenti letinyenti tabo thishela letivulekile ikakhu lukati beTibalo neSayensi.
