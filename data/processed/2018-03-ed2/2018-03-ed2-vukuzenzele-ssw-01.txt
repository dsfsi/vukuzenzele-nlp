luhlElO lw AhuluMENDE lolungasiti nje kuphela kucinisekisa kutsi tiphatsimandla tendzawo ticeceshwe kepha lugucula timphilo tebantfu labasha.

More Matshediso

Kukhula ekhaya le liphuyile leliphe tfwe ngumake longu mtali munye kuba yintfo lenemkhawulo kepha loko akutange kumvimbe Vele Mukhodiwa ekulandzeleleni liphupho lakhe lekuba ngu mhlelidolobha.
Mukhodiwa, longuweku dzabuka esigodzini sase Luheni kuMasipala We ndzawo waseThulamela  eLimpopo, watfola Ticu te Urban and Regional Planning  eNyuvesi yaseVenda nga2016.
Ungulomunye webazu zi beluhlelo lweBapho tfuli Labancane lwe-Eje nti Lesekela Sakhiwonchanti saMasipala (i-MISA), loluhlose kutsi lifunele bapho tfuli labaneticu misebe nti nematfuba ekuceceshwa besemsebentini  emikha kheni yetebucwepheshe le fanele kute kutsi babhaliswe njengetingcweti.
I-MISA luphiko lwahulumende wavelonkhe le lingekhatsi kweLitiko  Letekubusa Ngekubambisana. Yasungulwa ngeNkhwe khweti 2012.
“Ngemuva kwekuphotfu la matikuletjeni nga-2008, kwadzingeka kutsi ngihlale umnyaka munye ekhaya ngi ngafundzi ngibukene ne kutsi ngitayitfola kuphi i mali yekukhokhela inyuve si,” kwasho Mukhodiwa.
Nga-2010, wabhalisa eNyu vesi yaseJozi kepha ngenca yekweswelakala kwetimali akatange akhone kucedza umnyaka wakhe wekucala kwadzingeka kutsi ayishiye phansi inyuvesi.
Ngemnyaka lolandzelako, wabhalisela ticu te-Electrical Engineering N3  ekolishi lendzawo kepha masinyane nje wakubona loko kutsi aku siyo intfo yakhe.
Waphindze-ke futsi wacala phansi wa tfumela ticelo emanyuvesi ngelitsemba lekutsi utawu tfola lusito lwemali.
Ngenhlanhla, iNyuvesi ya seVenda yasemukela sicelo sakhe wabese ucala ticu ta khe te-Urban and Regional Pla nning nga-2012.
“Sikimu Savelonkhe Selusi to Lwetimali Tetitjudeni (i-NSFAS) yakhokhela tifu ndvo temnyaka wami weku cala kwase kutsi i-MISA yangi sita ngemfundzate nasenge nta umnyaka wami wesibili yaphindze futsi yangesekela ngate ngaphotfula tifundvo tami nga-2016,” kwasho yena.
Lomfundzate wakhokhela tonkhe tidzingo temfundvo, kufaka ekhatsi imali yekufundza, emabhuku nekwe kubhalela, indzawo yekuhla la nekudla.
Nga-2017 Mukhodiwa wa -cala kuceceshelwa umsebe nti kuMasipala weNdzawo iPrince Albert kuLitiko Letemisebenti yeMmango Nese kela Litiko.
“Kute kushiywa sikhatsi kangangobe ungete wafika lapho ufuna kufika khona e mphilweni. Ufanele ku tsi wente konkhe lokuse mandleni akho kutsi untjintje simo sakho uphidze futsi u ntjintje timo takho tekuphi la," Mukhodiwa.
Luhlelo lweBaphotfu li Labancane lwe-MISA  kwamanje lunebaphotfuli labange-70, kufaka ekhatsi bahlelidolobha nebahlelisigodzi labange-31, nabonjinela bagezi labange-24, bonjinela betekwakha laba-12 nebaphatsi labatsatfu betekwakha nemiklamo. Bonkhe bakubomasipala kuto tonke tifundza letiyimfica njengencenye yaloluhlelo.
Libambela Lesikhulu Le-siphetse (i-CEO) se-MISA  Ntandazo Vimba utsite lihhovisi lakhe liyasivisisa sidzingo lesikhulu sebantfu labanemakhono obunjinela newekwakha eNingizimu Afrika.
“Asifuni nje kuphela bantfu labasha labaneticu kule mikhakha, kepha sidzinga kucinisekisa kutsi batfola kuceceshwa lokusezingeni lemave emhlaba kantsi futsi batingcweti letibhalisiwe,” kwasho Vimba.
“Bomasipala betfu banesidzingo lesikhulu kakhu lu setingcweti. Sinesibopho lesikhulu sekwenta hulumende wendzawo asebente ngebungcweti, ngemitamo yetfu yekuhlomisa tisebenti ngemakhono," wengeta.


Batali bafanele kutsi badlale indzi ma lenkhulu elukhetfweni lwe-SGB

BAtAlI BANElItfuBA lekudlala indzima lehamba embili ekwenganyelweni kwetikolwa tahulumende ngenhloso yekwenta ncono emazinga nekwenta sikolwa sibe yindzawo lencono yebantfwana babo.

lukhetfo lwemti mba longamela siko lwa (i-SGB) lubanjwa ngeNdlovulenkhulu futsi batali bacelwa kutsi bahla nganyele kute kutsi badla le indzima lenemdlandla emfundvweni yebantfwana babo.
Lukhetfo lutawubanjwa kuto tonkhe tikolwa tahulumende kulo lonkhe lelive.
Indvuna yeLitiko Lete mfundvo Lesisekelo Angie Motshekga utsi lolu lukhetfo lolukhulu kakhulu kulelive, ngaphandle kwelukhetfo lwahulumende wendzawo nelukhetfo lwavelonkhe.  “Lukhetfo lwe-SGB lufaka sandla ekutfutfukeni umcondvo lonemandla webuniyo bemmango. Kuse benta kwetikolwa kuvamise kuba ngulokuncono uma ngabe batali bahlanga nyela ngalokunemdlandla  baphindze futsi banake netindzaba tesikolwa.”Utsite i-SGB inendzima lenkhulu lefanele kutsi iyi dlale ekulinganiseni ti ntfo letifunwa ngubo bonkhe labatsintsekako kwesekela umgomo munye lofananako wekutfola imiphumela ye mfundvo lekahle lephendvu la tidzingo temmango neku tfutfuka kwemnotfo.  I-SGB imelela yonkhe imi khakha yesikolwa, kufa ka ekhatsi batali, bothi shela, tisebenti letingafu ndzisi nebafundzi.  Sikha tsi sekuba sesikhundleni se-SGB yiminyaka lemitsa tfu.
Emandla ekuphatfa sikolwa Umtsetfo weTikolwa wase Ningizimu Afrika unika ema-SGB emandla labonaka lako ekwengamela tikolwa njengencenye yesakhi wo lesibalulekile sentsandvo yelinyenti.
Ngekuhlanganyela kulenchubo yelukhetfo, batali bacinise -kisa kutsi sikolwa sisebentela kahle kakhulu tintfo letifunwa bantfwana babo.
Nanoma nje emalunga e-SGB  akadzingeki kutsi abe neticu temfundvo, batali labanema khono ekugcinweni kwema bhuku, ekubalweni kwetimali kanye nakutinsinta temtsetfo ikakhulu kanye nalabo laba-nesifiso, futsi labanenshise-kelo ngemfundvo, bacelwa kutsi bacabange ngekungenela lolukhetfo.
