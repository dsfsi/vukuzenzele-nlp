I-NHI seyifike kutekunakekelwa ngetemphilo letincono

vukuzenzele unnamed

Indvuna yeTemphilo  Dkt. Aaron Motsoaledi utsi Umtsetfosivivinyo wekuChibela Tindzaba te Mshwalensi waVelonkhe we Temphilo (i-NHI) utawenta kancono lizinga letinsita te temphilo eNingizimu Afrika.
Motsoaledi bekakhuluma emhlanganweni webetindza ba losandza kubanjwa lapho beketfula khona Umtsetfo sivivinyo wekuChitjelwa kwetindzaba te-NHI kanye neMtsetfosivivinyo wekuChi bela tindzaba teTikimu teTe kwelashwa.LeMitsetfosivivinyo ivula indlela yekutsi babe banengi bantfu labatfola tinsita tete mphilo ngekusebentisa i-NHI.
Umtsetfosivivinyo luhlaka lwemtsetfo. Imitsetfosivivi nyo leminyeti yentiwa litiko lahulumende ngekucondzi swa yindvuna lefanele. LoMtse tfosivivinyo ufanele kutsi ubese uvunywa yiKhabhine thi ngembi kwekutsi wetfulwe ePhalamende.
Lokwetfulwa kwaleMitse tfosivivinyo lemibili kusho kutsi iNigizimu Afrika seyi dvute ngesinyatselo sinye ekutsini itfole tekunakekelwa kwebantfu ngelizinga lelihle letemphilo ngalokufananako lokusho kutsi-ke masinyane nje i-NHI itawuba ngumtse tfo.
I-NHI iyinchubo yekusita ngetimali letawenta sicinise ko sekutsi tonkhe takhamuti taseNingizimu Afrika titfola tinsita tetemphilo letibalule kile, ngekungakhatsali ngeti mo tabo temsebenti kanye ne kutsi bayakhona yini kufaka imali ngco kulesikhwama.
Motsoaledi utsite hulu mende uyati kutsi kukhona bantfu labakholelwa eku tsini kungakacalwa nje kutsi kukhulunywe nge-NHI, ku nesidzingo sekutsi kucalwe ngekulungisa simo setinsita tetemphilo letintengantenga ko saseNigizimu Afrika.
Utsite hulumende uyatati letinkinga telizinga lelibi ne kweswelakala kwemakhono kutekunakekelwa ngetemphi lo tahulumende.
Mtsoaledi utsite Luhlelo  Lwekutfutfukisa Lwavelonkhe  (i-NDP) yakucaphela loku  nekutsi futsi ekufezekiswe ni kwale-NHI, kudzingeka kutsi kulungiswe lelizinga le libi letinsita tetemphilo kanye netindleko tetinsita tete mphilo letibitwa tikhungo tete mphilo letitimele.
Ngesikhatsi kusasebanjwe tinkhulumomphikiswano  mayelana netichibelo tale Mitsetfosivivinyo, lelitiko lita wufezekisa lokungenani imi klamo lemine ye-NHI, lefa ka ekhatsi tinsita tetemphilo etikolweni, tinsita tetemphi lo talabagula ngengcondvo, tinsita tetemphilo tabomake labakhulelwe labanebumati ma nekukhulelwa kwabo eti bhedlela letinge-22 letitsintse ke kakhulu kanye newesi fo semdlavuta (i-oncology), ngekusita kakhulu iGau teng neKwaZulu-Natal, ko dvwa-ke kube kungabekwa eceleni letinye tifundza.
Motsoaledi uphindze watsi tindleko letibitwa tikhungo temphilo letitimele atifinyele lwa takhamuti taseNingizimu Afrika letinyenti. Sekubekwe loyo bekakadze aLijaji leLikhu lu Sandile Ngcobo yiKho mishini yeTekuncintisana ku tsi ente lucwaningo ngetindle ko tetinsita tetemphilo leti bitwa tikhungo tetemphilo letitimele.
“Letichibelo lesitetfula ko tentelwe kuletsa lusito lolu dzingeka kakhulu kutigu lane letititfola tikuletinkhulu tinkinga tekubabete imali,” kwasho yena.
Motsoaledi utsite sichibe lo sekucala saloMtsetfosivi vinyo sitawucedza kukhokha ngekuhlanganyela noma-ke kutsi kube khona imali nawe loyikhiphako ekhikhini lakho.
Kukhokha ngekubambisana kusho kutsi sikimu sikhokha incenye yenkhokhelo yema li sibhedlela noma dokotela lotimele layikhokhisa sigu lane. Lena lesele-ke imali ibese ifanela kutsi ikhokhwe ngulesigulane ngeyaso imali lephuma ikhikhini laso.
