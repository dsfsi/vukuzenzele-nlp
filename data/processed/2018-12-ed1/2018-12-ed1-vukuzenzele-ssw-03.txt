Sebentisa imali ngebuhlakani

Allison Cooper

Sikhatsi semaholide aKhisimusi akufuneki sishiye kuvuleke u mgodzi wetikweleti.
“Kusebentisa imali ngalo kwendlulele kufana nesi kweleti lesikukhatsatako le siviwa nguwe wedvwa. Ku ncono kakhulu kugwema kusebentisa imali ngalokwe ndlulele kuloKhisimusi ngo ba kungaba nemtselela lo mkhulu emalini yakho  isikhatsi lesidze,” kwasho umphatsi wetekutsenga nekutsengisa weDebtSafe Ma tthys Potgieter.
“Bumnandzi bekusebenti -sa imali ngalokwendlulele ngeLweti nangeNgongoni yintfo yesikhashana letawe nta batsengi badvonse mati ma ngenca yetincumo leba ngakaticabangisisi labati tsetse ngasekupheleni kwa Bhimbidvwane 2019. Ungase bentisi imali longenayo,” kwangeta Potgieter.
Mellony Ramalho we-Afri can Bank watsi tisebenti ti vamise kuholelwa ngembi kwesikhatsi ngeNgongoni, lokwenta sikhatsi sekulindza kuhola ngaBhimbidvwane sibe sidze.
“Bhimbidvwane yinyanga lematima ebantfwini labani ngi, labanye bagcina base bentisa ema-credit card noma baboleke imali. Kubalulekile kuhlala ucaphele kuloko losebentisa imali kuko. Ku ncono kwenta luhlu lwetinto lotawusebentisa imali kuto kute ubalekele kusebentisa imali ngalokwendlulele,” kwasho Ramalho.
Potgieter ucwayisa batse ngi kutsi bacale kutsenga tintfo tangeNgongoni kusanesikhatsi futsi bacinisekise kutsi banekudla lokwanele futsi banetinhlobo teku dla letitawuhlala sikhatsi lesidze, kute bakhone kulawula indlela lebayise bentisa ngayo imali yabo nekugwema kutsenga sekute sikhatsi tintfo setibita kakhulu.Potgieter wecwayise batsengi nganati tindlela labangayonga ngato imali: •	Batsenge kudla lokuningi lokutawuhlala sikhatsi lesidze.
•	Babuke kutsi angeke bagibelisane emotweni leyodvwa. Kusebentisa timoto letimbalwa kusho kutsi kutawusebenta imali lencane kuphethilomu.
•	Gwema kusebentisa imi gwaco lekhokhelwako uma kukhonakala.
•	Bantfwana abasebentise tintfo labatisungulele tona kwenta tipho tetihlobo kunekutsi titsengwe.
•	Cala kusanesikhatsi ku tsengela Khisimusi.
Lomnyaka licembu leDebt Safe ligcugcutela batsengi kutsi basebentise liphepha leletayelekile lelinsundvu kugocotela tipho, lelitsenge kako futsi lelingenabungoti kundalo.
