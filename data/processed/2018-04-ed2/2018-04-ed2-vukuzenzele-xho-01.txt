Ukulwa imbalela enkulu

vukuzenzele unnamed

Xa kulapha imbalela ifikelele kwawona maqondo aphezulu eyona ngxaki eMntla nase Mpuma Koloni apho amanzi emadamini kuMasipala oMba xa weNelson Mandela Bay (eBhayi) akwiqondo leepe senti ezi-25 kuphela,  lixesha ngoku lokuba abemi base Mzantsi Afrika bamanyane ba londoloze lo mthombo unqa bileyo noxabiseke kangaka.
Imbalela lixesha elide apho imvula ina kancinci ngendlela engaqhelekanga khona, nto leyo ekhokelela ekunqongophaleni kwamanzi.
Ngokutsho kukaGqirha uMathieu Roualt, injingalwa zi yaseYunivesithi yaseKapa kwiSebe lezeNzululwazi ngo Lwandle, izinto eziphambili ezi bangela imbalela lifuthe lenka nyamba u-El Niño nokutshi ntsha kwemozulu.  “Inkanyamba u-El Niño sisehlo esindaliweyo nesimana sibuya qho emva kweminyaka emithathu ukuya kwemihlanu. Unyusa amaqondo obushushu kumalwandle ePasifiki nawe Indiya, nto leyo kwelayo icala enokubangela iimeko zembale la,” utsho.
Ukutshintsha kwemozulu yimo echazwa njengoku tshintsha kwiindlela zenkqubo zemozulu ehlabathini liphela okanye kummandla. Yinto leyo eyaqala phakathi ukuya ekupheleni kwenkulungwane yama20, yaye ibangwa ikakhulu ngamaqondo aphezulu omoya  omdaka olapha esibhakabhakeni nozalwa zizibaso zezinto ezaziphila mandulo zaza zaba ngamatye.
“Kule nkulungwane id luleyo, amazantsi e-Afri ka abe nokubaxeka ko kutshintsha kwemozulu unyaka nonyaka, nto leyo ekhokelele kwi mbalela enkulu noku phazamiseka kwezitya lo nezinto eziphilayo elwandle nasehlabathi ni. Oku kutshintsha kwemozulu kunefuthe kushishino lwezolimo, oovimba bamanzi nezokuloba kuze kugqi bele ngoqoqosho ngokubanzi.
Oku kutshintsha kukwacha phazela nokuhamba kwamanzi emachibini, izityalo nokungena kwezondlo elwandle,” uchaze watsho uGqirha Roualt.
Eli futhe liviwa kakhulu ngokukodwa nguMzantsi Afri ka kuba ilizwe lethu kakade selinomyinge ophantsi kakade wokuna kwemvula ngonyaka oziimililitha ezingama-450, xa kuthelekiswa nomyinge wehla -bathi oziimililitha ezingama-860.  Liyintoni ifuthe lembalela?Ngenyanga yoMdumba, iSebe loLawulo lweNtsebenziswano neMicimbi yeMveli layihlela ngokutsha imbalela layisusa kwimo eyintlekele yephondo yayintlekele yesizwe. “Emva kokuhlola ngokutsha ubungakanani nobukhali be mbalela egqubayo ubuncinane kumaphondo amathathu, ab alawuli bentlekele baphinda bayihlela ngokutsha imbalela njengentlekele yesizwe,”  itshilo intloko yeZiko loLawulo lweNtlekele leSizwe, uGqirha Mmaphaka Tau.  Noxa ifuthe lembalela kwe zoqoqosho kunzima ukulili nganisa, libe likhulu kakhulu kwicandelo lezolimo. ENtsho na Koloni, eliliphondo lesibini elikhulu kweli lizwe  elinegalelo kwisambuku semveliso yesizwe (i-GDP), yimisebenzi engama 21 000 eyeyaphela kwikota ye sithathu kulo nyaka uphelileyo.  Imveliso yengqolowa yehle ngeepesenti ezingama-47 kowa ma-2017 yaye kungakho uku silela okunokuba ngangeelitha ezizigidi ezingama-90 zewayini enokuthunyelwa kumazwe angaphandle nenokuthengiswa apha ekhaya.  Ukunqongophala kwamanzi kuza kuphinda kube nefuthe eli buhlungu kugutyulo, unono phelo lwezempilo nokhenketho.
Yintoni eyenziwayo ukuhlangabezana nale meko?Oomasipala abantlithwe yimba lela babekele abahlali namashi shini izithintelo ezingqongqo zokuseyenziswa kwamanzi.  Izikhululo zokufunxa ityuwa emanzini, iinkqubo zokulungi swa kwemibhobho, ukuphinda kusetyenziswe kwamanzi abese le esetyenzisiwe nokutsala amanzi emhlabeni zonke zichongwe njengezisombululo zale meko, kwaye le misebenzi kungoku nje ikumanqanaba awohlukeneyo okuya ekugqityweni.
