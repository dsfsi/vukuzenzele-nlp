Abosomabubulo abanzima bamakhe mikhali beFreyistata bavula amathu ba wemisebenzi 

More Matshediso

I kampani esendaweni ye-Harrismith ngesi fundeni seFreyistata isiza epini yokulwa nokutlho geka kwemisebenzi ngoku vula amathuba wemisebenzi enzinzileko ema-70.
I-Kevali Chemicals iyikampani yokuthoma ephethwe babantu abanzima eFreyistata ekhiqiza amakhemikhali. Ikhiqiza ama khemikhali ahlukahlukene ko wokuhlwengisa amanzi, wokuhlanzekisa ngemakhaya nangeendlwaneni zangasese begodu nedlhu yokunamathi sela.
IKampani le yasungulwa ngomnyaka wee-2014 babantu abahlanu ababangani abane lwazi ngezamakhemikhali, ngezepahla ethingiseka msinyana, ngezokusungulwa kom khiqizo omutjha nokukhiqiza idlhu yokunamathisela.UmNqophisi oPhetheko wa kwa-Kevali Chemicals, uFune ka Khumalo uveza bona i-Black Industrialist Scheme isekele ikampanakhe ngokuyithengela imitjhini amathulusi neense tjenziswa zokusebenza.
Ungezelela ngokuthi “ekutho meni sabona kuqakathekile si fake imali yokuthoma irhwebo kodwana ngetjhudu ekugcineni safunyana isizo leemali emNyangweni wezeRhwebo namaBubulo (i-dti) ngokuyele liswa yi-Black Industrial Scheme (i-BIS) neHlangano eThuthukisa amaBubulo (i-IDC).
I-BIS lihlelo lesikhwama seemali somThethokambiso waboSomabubulo abaNzima elenzelwe ukuvulela abosoma bubulo abanzima beSewula Afrika amathuba emnothweni ngokubanikela isekelo ngeemali namkha ngokubathengela iinse tjenziswa elihleleke ngendlela ezabazuzisa ngefanelo.
UKhumalo uveza bona uku-thoma irhwebo kudle pheze iingidi ezili-18 zamaranda begodu kuthethe iinyanga ezi-li-11 ukobana ikampani yenze inzuzo.
Ikampani isabalalise imikhi qizwayo eSewula Afrika ma zombe neenarheni ezinje-Uga nda, Namibia ne-Tanzania.
UKhumalo uhlathulula bona umthelela omuhle wekampani le emaphilweni wabahlali bekhethwepha mkhulu uku dlula ukuvula kwayo amathuba wemisebenzi.
 “Ngaphezu kokuvulela izakhamuzi amathuba wemisebenzi, sinamahlelo wokwenyula umphakathi emirarweni yezehlalakuhle nangokusekela abantu bengubo bemalokitjhini nebeendaweni zemakhaya abahlela ukuvula amarhwebo amancani ngamakghono abanawo wokukhiqiza ama khemikhali wokuhlwengisa ngemakhaya nangeendlwaneni zangasese”, ubeke watjho.
Uveza bona abosomabubulo abasakhasako kumele bazi bonyana akusilula ukuthoma irhwebo kodwana kufanele bangapheli iinhliziyo ngombana ngokukhamba kwesikhathi bazokufumana umvuzo woku zikhandla kwabo.
ISekela likaNgqongqotjhe we-dti uBulelani Magwanishe uthe, iKevali ifanele ihlonitjhwe ngokuba yikampani yokuthoma esemkhakheni wokukhi qiza amakhemikhali, iinhlahla neemplastiki ukobana isekelwe ngeemali ngaphasi kweHlelo laboSomabubulo abaNzima uku sukela ngomnyaka wee-2016.
 “IKampani le izokuvula ama thuba wemisebenzi etlhogwa khulu babantu bemiphaka thini ezombeleze i-Harrismith labo ababanjwe mtlhago woku ngasebenzi,’’ kutjho iSekela likaNgqongqotjhe uMagwa nishe.
