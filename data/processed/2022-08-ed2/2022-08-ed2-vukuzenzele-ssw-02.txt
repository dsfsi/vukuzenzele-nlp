Ematfuba emfundzate kubantfu labasha

Kgaogelo Letsebe 

Sonia Malinga (23) wasePiet Retief eMpumalanga utsi kutfola umfundzate lovela ku-Shoprite Group kucinisekise kutsi utawu ba nelikusasa lelitsembi sako.
Malinga utfole lomfu ndzate kulesitolo se kutsengisa saseNingi zimu Afrika ngemnyaka we-2018. Ufake sicelo salomfundzate emva kwekuphotfula imfundvo yemabanga lasetulu  e-Hoerskool Piet Retief.
“Ngisakhula, ngacala ngafuna kuba ngudo kotela, kodvwa kamuva ngacala kubuka letinye tintfo. Ngafundza ngete luchungechunge lokwe tfulwa kwetinsita kanye netekulawula kuhanjiswa kwemphahla futsi loku kwekugcina kwacishe kwangidvonsa ngaleso sikhatsi. Ngiyakhumbu la bengivamise kuhlala ngibuka emaloli angena futsi aphuma edolobheni langakitsi bese ngiyatibu ta kutsi ngubani locinise kisa kutsi letintfo letitfu tswako tisezingeni futsi tilinani lelifanele yini nekutsi ingabe tiyafika yini endzaweni lekufa nele tiye kuyo. Kungaleso sikhatsi lapho ngacala kuba nelutsandvo lalo msebenti," kusho Ma linga.
Malinga watfola ticu teBCom Honours  Kute kulawula Kuhanjiswa Kwemphahla eNyuni vesi yaseJohannesburg wabese ucala kusebenta e-Shoprite njengemphatsi wesikhungo losafundza abe aceceshwa ngeNdlo vana 2021. Nyalo losesi usandza kukhushulelwa esikhundleni sekuba ngumhlatiyi weku gcwaliswa kabusha kwe mphahla.
Kuba yincenye yeShoprite Group kukhulise lwati laMalinga kule mboni yetekutsengisa.
“Kungifundzisile kakhulu futsi nangalokungetiwe mayelana netekulawula kuhanjiswa kwemphahla nangekungenta kutsi ngichubeke nekutfu tfukisa emakhono ami nangekutsi ngikhule emsebentini wami," usho njalo losesi.
Usifaka njani sicelo Kufaka ticelo teluhlelo lwemfundzate lofaka konkhe we-Shoprite Group kuvuliwe kumnya ka wekufundza we2023. Lomfundzate ucala ku-R45 000 uye ku-75 000 ngemnyaka, kuye ngemkhakha wekufundza nangaleso naleso sikhu ngo setemfundvo. Ufaka ekhatsi imali yekufundza,  yemabhuku, imali ye -kuhlala kusikhungo lofundza kuso kanye ne tivumelwano tekusebenta kubo uma sewuphotfulile.
Tidzingo letisisekelo tekutsi wamukelwe tifaka kutsi ube sakhamuti sa seNingizimu Afrika, ube uphotfule Matikuletjeni/Libanga 12 nekutsi  ungabi ngetulu kwemi nyaka le-27 budzala.
Imifundzate ikhona kulemikhakha lelandze lako futsi labafaka ticelo kufuneka babe kulomnya ka lobekiwe wekufundza ngalesikhatsi labafaka ngaso ticelo tabo:
• Tifundvo tePharmacy (umnyaka wekucala, wesibili, wesitsatfu newesine)• Tifundvo teRetail Busi ness Management  (umnyaka wekucala, wesibili newesitsatfu)• Tifundvo teAccounting (umnyaka wesibili, we sitsatfu newesine)• Tifundvo teE-commer ce: UI Design  (umnya ka wesibili newesi tsatfu) • Tifundvo teFood Scien ces (umnyaka wesibili, wesitsatfu newesine) • Tifundvo teInformation Technology (umnyaka wesibili newesitsatfu)• Tifundvo teLogistics and Supply Chain  (umnyaka wesitsatfu newesine).
