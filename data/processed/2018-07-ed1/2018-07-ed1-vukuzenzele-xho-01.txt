
Abantu abayivali imilomo ngumhlaba abawubuyiselweyo

Siya Miti 

Abantu baseMpuma Koloni baza kutha tha inxaxheba kwica ndelo lezilwanyana zasendle emva kokuba befumene ima-li kurhulumente yokuqalisa umyezo wezilwanyana zasendle oza kuba ngowoluntu.
Indawo yaseDouble Drift cebu kuhle eRhini inabantu aba li-1 500 abaphuma kumakhaya angama-264. Basuswa kumhlaba wabo ngurhulumente wase Ciskei ngowe-1991, kusenzelwa umyezo wezilwanyana zasendle wabantu abamhlophe.
Abantu balapha basanda kufumana iitayitile zalo mhlaba, obuyiselwe kubo ngenkqubo karhulumente yokubuyiswa komhlaba.
Ngokokutsho kweNkulumbuso yaseMpuma Koloni uPhumu lo Masualle, kubekelwe bucala imali ezizigidi ezingama-21 zee randi ukulungisela ukuthenga lo mhlaba kwaye uza kunikezelwa ebantwini ngezigaba ezahlu-keneyo.
"Eli linyathelo elibalulekileyo kumalinge olondolozo nawokuzisa iinguqu. Lo myezo wezilwa nyana zasendle uyalungiswa ukuze ube kumgangatho apho uza kukwazi ukukhuphisana neminye imiyezo yezilwanyana kwaye norhulumente uluxhasa ngemali, izilwanyana zasendle kunye noqeqesho uluntu lwa-lapha," utshilo uMasualle.
Abantu balapha, abawulawula kunye ne-Arhente yezoKhe nketho nemiYezo eMpuma Kolo ni (i-ECPTA) lo myezo phantsi kweLikhayalethu Community Property Association (i-LCPA), bafumene imali ezizigidi zee randi ezithandathu kwiSebe lezeNdalo yokuba baqalise ishi shini ngezilwanyana zasendle kulo myezo, uzihekthare ezili 1 400 ubukhulu, bawuthenge-lwe iSebe lezokuPhuhliswa kwamaPhandle noBuyekezo lwemiHlaba.
Usihlalo we-LCPA, uMxolisi Ngesi, uthi abantu balapha bafaka isicelo sokubuyiselwa umhla ba ngowe-1998. Ngowe-2012, baye banikwa umhlaba ozihe kthare ezingama-23 500 ekungo ku nje othe waphinda wonge zwa ngomyezo wezilwanyana zasendle ozihekthare ezili-1 400.
"Sivuya kakhulu kuba siyifumene yonke into ebesiyifu na. Urhulumente usincedise nangemali yokwakha iziseko zophuhliso. Sinazo izilwanyana zasendle, eziquka amaqwa rhashe ali-10, amaxhama ali-15 neempofu ezili-10," utshilo uNgesi, esongeza nokuba kuza kuthengwa iinyathi nee-impala kungekudala.
Uphinde wongeza ngelokuba abantu balapha bafumana uqeqesho kwi-ECPTA ngeendlela zokulawula nokuphatha umye zo wezilwanyana zasendle. "Izicwangciso zethu kukwakha ii-lodge ezimbini kodwa ngoku sele sisakha iindawo zokuhlala oonogada balo myezo kunye nobuhlanti bokothulela izilwa nyana ezifikayo," utshilo uNgesi.
"Ngethuba kubiywa ucingo kwindawo ezikhilomitha ezingama-10 kunye nesango lokungena, kudalelwe abantu abalapha izithuba zemisebenzi ezingama-61," utshilo uMphathiswa wezeNdalo u-Ednah Molewa ngethuba lo myezo uni kezelwa ngokusesikweni.
