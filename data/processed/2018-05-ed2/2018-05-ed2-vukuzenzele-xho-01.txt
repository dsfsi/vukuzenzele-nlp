Uxanduva loMzantsi Afrika lokufundisa ulutsha

More Matshediso

UMphathiswa weMfu ndo ePhakami leyo noQeqesho uNa ledi Pandor uthi uMzantsi Afrika unako ukubonele la ngeebhasari abantwana beentsapho ezihlupheki leyo nabaphuma kumakhaya amkela imivuzo ephantsi abafunda kwiiyunivesithi nakwiikholeji zeMfundo noQeqesho lobuChule no buGcisa (i-TVET) ngowama -2018.
UMphathiswa uPandor uthe luxanduva lwelizwe ukuxhasa abantu abangamahlwempu kunye nabo bangathathi  ntweni.
Ebethetha ngethuba lendi bano noonondaba apho athe wathi thaca indlela eza kuqa -liswa ngayo inkqubo yemfu ndo ephakamileyo yasimahla eMzantsi Afrika.
UMphathiswa uPandor ubhe ngeze ukuba uRhulumente ugalele enye imali ezibhiliyoni eziyi-7.166 zeerandi ukuze iye kwiibhasari zabantwana aba phuma kumakhaya ahlupheki leyo namkela imivuzo ephantsi abafunda kwiiyunivesithi nakwiikholeji zoQeqesho lobuGcisa kunye neMfundo yezoQeqesho (i-TVET) kulo nyaka.
UMphathiswa uPandor uqinisekisile ukuba imali ezi bhiliyoni eziyi-4.581 zeerandi ibekelwe bucala ukulungise lela abafundi abayifaneleyo abafunda eyunivesithi yaze imali ezibhiliyoni eziyi-2.585 zeerandi yabekelwa abafundi abafunda kwiikholeji ze-TVET.
Ngowama-2017 urhulumente wamemezela ukuba imfu ndo ephakamileyo iza kuba simahla kubafundi abahlu phekileyo kunye nabo bavela kumakhaya amkela umvuzo wonyaka ohlangeneyo oya kutsho kuma-350 000 eerandi.
Abafundi baxhaswa ngenkqubo yebhasari eyandisi weyo, ethatha indawo yema li-mboleko yangaphambili kunye nenkqubo enesixa esiyi bhasari ebinikezelwa siSikimu seSizwe soNcedo lwezeMali sabaFundi (i-NSFAS).  “Ulwabiwo olusisiseko oluya kwi-NSFAS lokuxhasa abafu ndi abahluphekayo nabo ba phuma kumakhaya amkela imi vuzo ephantsi abafunda kwii yunivesithi kunye ne-TVET luza kwanda ukusuka kwimali ezibhiliyoni eziyi-9.849 zee randi ngowama-2017/18 ukuya kwiibhiliyoni ezinga ma-35.321 zeerandi ngowa ma-2020/21.
“Oku kuthetha ukuba kufune ka ziphucuke iinkqubo ze-NS FAS kunye nendlela asebenza ngayo. Ngoko ke siye sabekela bucala enye ima-li ezizigidi ezili-105 zeerandi kwe si sithuba seNkqubo-sikho kelo yeNkcitho yesiGaba  esiPhakathi ukunceda i-NSFAS ukuba yandise kwaye iqinise amandla ayo okulawula,"  utshilo uMphathiswa.
Abafundi abafanelekileyo, nokuba benza owuphi unyaka,  abafunda kwiikholeji ze-TVET zikarhulumente baza kufuma na ibhasari eyanele imali yabo yokufunda kunye nezinto zokufunda.
Ukunyuswa kwemali ngowa ma-2018/19 kuza kuqinisekisa ukuba abafundi abangama-458 875 baza kufumana iibhasari.Abafundi abamalunga nama50 480 abafunda kwiikholeji zeTVET, abafanelekileyo ukuba bafumane imali yokubancedisa ngezinto zokukhwela, indawo yokuhlala kunye nokutya, baza kuxhaswa nabo; ngelixa abanye abangama-82 600 beza kufumana izibonelelo zothutho.
UMphathiswa uthe esi sixa sitsha semali kulindeleke ukuba sixhase inani leendawo zokufunda ezimalunga nama83 200 kwezi zingama-208 000 (ama-40%) zikhoyo zabafu ndi abatsha kwiiyunivesithi ngowama-2018.
