I-NHI Iligadango Elinqophe Ukuthuthukisa Zamaphilo

vukuzenzele unnamed

UNgqongqotjhe we zamaPhilo uDorh.  Aaron Motsoaledi u thi umThethomlingwa oKhitje lelwako weTjhorensi yezama Philo yeliZweloke (i-NHI) uzokukhuphula izinga lesizo lezamaphilo eSewula Afrika.
U-Motsoaledi gade akhulu ma mhlapha emhlanganweni weembikiindaba lapho ethule isiKhibelelo somThethomli ngwa we-NHI nesiKhibele lo somThethomlingwa wee Nkimu zezokweLapha.
ImiThethomlingwa le izoku hlahla indlela yokufikelela zokwelapha lula ngaphasi kwehlelo le-NHI.
Umthethomlingwa litlha tlha lomthetho ohlongo zwako. ImiThethomlingwa eminengi itlanywa minya ngo karhulumende ngelayelo likaNgqongqotjhe womnya ngo loyo. Kufuze umThetho mlingwa uphasiswe yiKhabi nethi ngaphambi kokobana uthunyelwe ePalamende.
Ukwethulwa kwemiThetho mlingwa emibili le kutjho iragelophambili leSewula Afrika ekufikeleleni isizo lezamaphilo labo boke aba ntu okutjho bona i-NHI izoku ba mthetho msinyana.
I-NHI lihlelo elisekela  ngeemali elizokuqinisekisa bona zoke izakhamuzi ze Sewula Afrika zinikelwa itjhe jo lezamaphilo ezilitlhogako, kungayi ngobujamo bomse -benzabo nokukghona ukuzi bhadelela.
UDorh. Motsoaledi uthe urhulumende uyelele bona abanye abantu bajame kelo kuthi kufuze kulungiswe qangi ihlelo lezamaphilo lo mphakathi ngaphambi koku thi bekukhulunywe nge-NHI.
Uthe urhulumende uyazazi iintjhijilo zezinga elibogabo gako nokungasebenzi nge fanelo kwehlelo lezamaphilo womphakathi.
UDorh. Motsoaledi uthe umTlamo wokuThuthukiswa kweliZweloke (i-NDP) uku tshwaye ngaphandle koku zaza ukuthi ngokuphunyele liswa kwe-NHI iinsiza zezama philo ezibogabogako neemali ezitjhisako zesizo lezamaphilo langeqadi kufuze kulungiswe.
Kusakhulunyiswana ngee nKhibelelo zemiThethomli ngwa le, umnyango uzokusu ngula okungasenani nga maphrojekthi amane azokufa ka hlangana isizo lezamaphilo eenkolweni, isizo lezamaphi lo kabaphazamiseke ngoko mkhumbulo, abomma abasi disi abanemiraro yezamaphilo eembhedlela ezima-22 ezithi nteke khulu nangokwelatjhwa kwekankere, i-oncology  ku tjhejwe khulukhulu ukusiza e-Gauteng naKwaZulu-Nata la, nokho-ke ezinye iimfunda azikazukuliselelwa.
UDorh. Motsoaledi uthe izakhamuzi ezinengi azisa kghoni ukufikelela iinsiza zezamaphilo zangeqadi  ngonobangela wokuphaka ma khulu kweemali zeensiza lezo, ngebangelo iKomitjhini yezamaPhaliswano ikhombele iJaji eliKhulu langaphambilini uSandile Ngcobo ukobana enze iphenyo ngeemali zee nsiza zezamaphilo zangeqadi kilomkhakha.
Uthe “Iinkhibelelo esizethu lako zinqophe ukwethula iingulani umthwalo weemali ezizifumana zikiwo”.
U-Motsoaledi uthe isikhi belelo sokuthoma somThe thomlingwa kuzakuba nge sokuphelisa iimali ezibhade lwa ziingulani kibodorhodera ngaphezu kwemali ekhutjhwa eenkimini zabo zokwelatjhwa.
Iimali ezibhadelwa ziingu lani zitjho bonyana iskimu sibhadela ingcenyana yema li ebizwe sibhedlela namkha ngudorhodera kuthi enye ibhadelwe sigulani. Imali eseleko kufuze iphume esi khwanyeni sesigulani ngo kwaso.
