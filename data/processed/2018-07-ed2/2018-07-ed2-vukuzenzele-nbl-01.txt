Ipilo Nayikuvulela Amathuba, Asebenzise

Amukelani Chauke

UmNyango we zokuLima weTji ngalanga Kapa ula wula ihlelo elisiza abalimi abasakhasako nesele babo makekere ngamano ukusu kela ngelanga lokuthoma ukuba balimi – nabatlhoga isizo leemali – ukufikela lapho bakghona ukuzijamela ngokwabo.
IHlelo lokuSekela noku Thuthukisa abaLimi lisize ilutjha elifana no-Wayne Mansfield (oneminyaka ema-33) odabuka e-Paarl, ogade athengisela uso nghwanakhe eMarageni weKapa ngamalanga laya asafunda isikolo wazoku ba mlimi okhiqiza abosuru enarheni ayiqatjhileko abese ubathumela emazweni wa ngaphetjheya.
Eminyakeni elitjhumi eya dlulako, u-Mansfield nagade amrhwebi osakhasako one minyaka ema-23, waziphu ndisa ngethuba lokusebe nza eplasini lapho beka funyana namkha beka thenga khona abosuru  azobathengisa. Unobange la walokhu kukobana waye gade azibona asesemncani begodu anganakareko ke zokulima. Ngemva kwemi nyakana watjhugulula u mkhumbulwakhe nakabona isidingo esikhulako sabosu ru.
Wathi, “Ngayelela bona abalimi benza okwenziwa barhwebi – bebazipakela istoko sabo bazithengisele emarageni weKapa. Ngaye lela bona ngokukhamba kwesikhathi thina esithengi sa endleleni asizukufumana litho”.
Ngeminyaka yee-2013, umlimi wendawo wanza isivumelwano sokuthwasi sa u-Mansfield kezokulima, wamqatjhisela amahekthara ali-12 wenarha. Wafaka neensetjenziswa zokusezela okwaba mthangalasisekelo olisizo ku-Mansfield uko bana alime.
Ngomnyaka wee-2015, wakghona ukuthumela ama thani wabosuru ama-31, kwathi ngomnyaka wee2016 isibalwesi sakhula khulu ukufikela kumathani ali-168.
Koke lokhu kwaba yipume lelo ngenca yokuthwasiswa nesizo alifumana eHlelweni lokuSekela nokuThuthuki sa abaLimi lapho afumana isibonelelo seHlelo eliHla nganyelweko seSekelo le zokuLima (i-CASP) ngo mnyaka wee-2015 ngalo kho wakghona ukunzi nzisa ibubulo lakhe bewa phumelela njengomlimi.
Ukuthuthukisa Abalimi NgamakghonoIhlelo lokuSiza nokuThu thukisa abaLimi linqophe ukuthuthukisa amakghono wabantu newemiphakathi ebegade idinywe amathuba ngaphambilini, khulukhulu abaZuzi eHlelweni lokuBu yiselwa Inarha.  Litjheja abalimi abasakhasako, abaziphilisa ngokulima, bamasimu amancani nabalima umthamo omkhulu ngomnqopho wo-kuthengisa eemarageni.
Umlawuli wephrojekthi yeziko lokuthuthukiswa kwezokulima emNyangweni wezokuLima eTjingalanga Kapa, uShaheed Martin wathi lelihlelo lisize abalimi bakghona ukuziphilisa.
Wathi, “Sisiza abalimi basathoma. Lokhu kutjho bona sibasiza ngekambi so yokwenza iimbawo zeemali, nabamukelweko, sibasiza bathome ukulima nokukhiqiza”.
Wahlathulula bona indlela esetjenziswa mnyango ngepheleleko netjheja zonke iindingo zabalimi begodu isekela ngemisebenzi yezokulima, kufaka hlangana ukusekela ngomthangalasisekelo.
Wathi, “Sinabosolwazi bezo mnotho ekungibo abazaku siza abalimi ngokubafunyanela iimaraga abazakuthengisa kizo imikhiqizwabo nasele banzinzile”.
