Ukubhalisela uvoto kuza kuba ngeyoKwi ndla yowama-2018

More Matshediso

I-IEC yoMzantsi Afrika yazise umjikelo woku qala wempelaveki yoku bhalisela uvoto kunyulo lwama-2019, kwaye ibo ngoze abavoti abafanele kileyo kweli liphela ukuba batyelele izikhululo zabo zovoto bahlaziye iinkcukacha zabo kumqulu woluhlu lwabavoti okanye babhalisele uvoto.
IGosa eliyiNtloko loNyulo lekomishoni, uSy Mama bolo, uthe zonke izikhu lulo zovoto ezingama-22 612 ziza kuvula ngempela veki yomhla we-10  nowe-11 kweyoKwindla yowama-2018 ukususela ngentsimbi ye-8 kusasa ukuya kweye-5 emva kwemini. Oku kwenzelwa okokuba kube lula kwaye kuncede abavoti bahlaziye iinkcukacha zabo zovoto okanye ba bhalise. Okokuba ufudukele kwenye indawo engeyiyo le wawuhlala kuyo ukugqibela kwakho ukuvota, kufune ka uphinde ubhalise kwesi sithili sakho sitsha sovoto.  Abemi boMzantsi Afrika abaminyaka ili-16 nangaphezulu  nabanesazisi esi semthethweni okanye  ikhadi bangabhalisela uvoto. Okokuba  akubhalisanga, aku sayi kuvunyelwa okokuba uvote ngowama-2019.
Abavoti ababhalisileyo abaneenkcukacha zeedilesi zabo ezingekhoyo kumqulu woluhlu lwabavoti, iinkcu kacha ezifunekayo bangazi nikezela kwizikhululo zabo zovoto xa kubhaliswa.
“Sifuna abavoti beze baze basebenzise eli thuba,”  utshilo uMamabolo. “IKomi shoni yoNyulo kungoku nje ayikabinazo nangoku iidilesi zabavoti abamalunga nezigidi ezi-2.8. Ngaphezu koko kukho iidilesi ezizigidi ezi-3.5 ezihlelwe “njengezi ngagqibekanga” okanye “ezi ngacacanga.” Olu didi lufuna ukuphinda luhlahlelwe ukuqinisekisa okokuba ngaba zikho na ezinye iinkcukacha ezikhoyo,” wongeze ngeli tshoyo.
Ikomishoni ifumene iidile si ngokusebenzisa amali nge awohlukeneyo aquka ukuhlaziya iingxelo, iifayi li neendawo zokulondolo za izibhalo ezibalulekileyo. Oku ikwenzela ukukhangela nokufumana iidilesi ezinikezelwe ngabavoti kwimi nyaka engama-20 edluleyo; ngexesha iqokelela iidilesi ngexesha lonyulo loomasipala lowama-2016; nangokwe nza uphando lwangapha ndle ngaphambi kweempe laveki zokubhalisela unyulo lovalo-sikhewu.
Apho kuyimfuneko khona, abavoti ekufumaniseke okokuba babhaliswe kwizithi li zovoto ezingachanekanga, basuswa apho ngokweCa ndelo 12 (1)(d) loMthetho woNyulo.
“Siye safumana saze sasebenzisa, imithombo yeedi lesi ekwamanye  amacande lo ombuso afana neZiko lee Nkcukacha-manani loMza ntsi Afrika (i-Statistics South Africa), i-Arhente yeZibonelelo zezeNtlalo noMhla la-phantsi yoMzantsi Afrika (i-SASSA), u-Telkom, iPosi yoMzantsi Afrika neSebe leMicimbi yaseKhaya, apho sikwaziyo khona,  ” utshilo.
