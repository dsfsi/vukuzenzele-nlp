Kunekghonakalo Yokungezelelwa Kwamalimi Asemthethweni eSewula Afrika

Neo Semono

Ilimi i-Khelobedu linga ba lilimi le-12 emalimini asemthethweni eSewula Afrika, ngokukhambisana nokubuyisa isithunzi sobu khosi babantu eSewula Afrika.
 UMengameli u-Cyril Rama phosa wenze isimemezelwesi nabekugcotjwa ngokwesigi yani iNdlovukazi ya-Ba lobedu eBaleni lezemiDlalo i-Mokwakwaila e-Bolobedu, Ga-Modjadji, e-Limpopo.

 I-Khelobedu lilimi elikhu lunywa sitjhaba sa-Balobedu, esizakubuswa yiNdlovukazi yeZulu ya-Balobedu ene minyaka eli-13 njenganje, nayiqeda ukugcotjelwa le sisikhundla sokuba yiNdlo vukazi u-Modjadji VII nayi hlanganisa iminyaka eli-18.
 Nakagcotjelwa isikhundlesi uzabe sele aqede ukukhuliswa  ngokwesiko la-Balobedu.“Leli-ke ligadango lama mbala lendima ehle yomla ndo uMma u-Winnie Madi kizela-Mandela abeka zazikhakhazisa ngalo. Lobu kuphela kobukhosi be Ndlovukazi obukhona eSe wula Afrika ne-Afrika jikele le,” kutjho u-Ramaphosa.
UMengameli u-Ramapho sa ukhulume waqinisele la ukuzibophelela kwaka rhulumende ekubuyiseni ukuhlonipheka nesithunzi sobukhosi babantu abanzima. UBukhosi ba-Balobedu bubuyiswe ngokusemthe thweni ngenyanga kaNtaka wee-2016, ngemva kwerhu bhululo elenziwa yiKomi tjhini YomBango WoBukho si (i-CTLDC) ngobujamo ba-Balobedu, njengobana kwakunombango bekwafa kwa isibawo serhubhululo kiyo i-CTLDC.
“URhulumende uzibo phelele ukuyiphumele lisa nokuyipha amandla  imibandela yeSahluko se12 somThethosisekelo,  khulu khulu ukubuyiswa kwesithunzi sobukhosi ba bantu ehlelweni lokuBuswa ngeNtando yeNengi,” kutjho uMengameli u-Ramaphosa.
UMengameli u-Ramaphosa uthe ukuhlonitjhwa kobu khosi beNdlovukazi namkha bomuntu omsikazi eSewula Afrika kutjengisa ukuzibo phelela ngekambisolawulo namandla wokulinganiswa kobulili, ukube ngahlanye kuhlonitjhwa nendima  yoburholi elinywa babantu abasikazi kiwo woke ama koro wepilo.  “Amakhosi anomsebenzi oqakatheke khulu ekufuze awenze ekwakheni umpha kathi onganamtlhago, onga nakutlhogeka komsebe nzi nongatjhiyani ngendima yepilo. Amakhosi wekhethu kufuze akhambe phambili emzabalazweni wokubuyisa ukuhlonitjhwa kwamalunge lo nesithunzi sabantu bekhe thu.”

 Ubuye wagandelela nokuqa katheka kwendima elinywa  makhosi ekubuyiseleni aba ntu iindawo namkha iinarha zabo.
“Kufanele benze isiqinise ko sokuthi inarha ibuyiselwa kilabo abayisebenzako, babe benze nesiqiniseko sokuthi nalabo ababuyiselwa inarha banawo amandla, iimfanelo kunye nelwazi lokuyisebenza inarha leyo,” kutjho uMenga meli u-Ramaphosa.
Balinganiselwa ema-25 we engidi abantu abahlala  eendaweni zemakhaya aba khele namkha abahlala ngaphasi  kwelawulo lama khosi.

Uthi Bewusazi Nje?
• A-Balobedu behlelwa liliwa elimbi ngomnyaka we-1972 mhlazana iNdlovukazi yeZulu yangesikhatheso u-Makoma Modjadji emukwa amandla wobukhosi bakhe, wehliselwa esikhundleni sokuba mdosiphambili wesiqhenyana namkha induna yesiqhenyana sabantu. 
• Iindawo neenduna ezingaphasi kwesandla sakhe zafakwa ngaphasi kwesabelo se-Lebowa nese-Gazankulu, ngaleyindlela-ke kwabulawa amasiko anothileko namafa wesiko la-Balobedu elineminyaka engaphezulu kwama-400.
