
Abakhenkethi bangaphandle abayivali imilomo zizibiliboco zeli

Dale Hes

Abahlali beeloki shi zikamasipala  waseGeorge eNtsho na Koloni baza kuphekela abakhenkethi bangaphandle ukutya nezinye izibiliboco ezimuncis'iintupha. Eli phulo liyinxalenye yephulo i-Dine with Locals.
Eli phulo liyinxalenye yokubhiyozela iNyanga ye zoKhenketho apho umasipa la akhethe amaqela amatha ndathu aphuma kwilokishi yaseThembalethu nasePa caltsdorp ukuba aphekele abakhenkethi bangaphandle ukutya okuhamba nazo zonke izimuncu-muncu, futhi aba ntende kangangoko.
Ngethuba la maqela esenza amalungiselelo okuntenda nokuphekela ezi ndwendwe athe afumana uqeqesho olu nxulumene nezokhenketho nobuchule bokulungisa uku tya.  Baye bafundiswa izakhono ezinxulumene nokuqala  kunye nokuqhuba ishishini, injongo yoku ikukuba ekugqi beleni babe ngoosomashishi ni abanengeniso encumisayo kwicandelo lezokhenketho.  UNomzamo Entile (one minyaka yobudala enga ma-31) oyinxalenye yala maqela mathandathu, uyi nkokeli ye-Explore our Hood Team yaseThembalethu. UNo mzamo waqala i-Explore our Hood ngenjongo yokukhu thaza ukubambisana noku hlalisana ngoxolo eGeorge.
"Saya saqaphela ukuba kukho ukungavani phakathi kwabantu baseGeorge. Sifuna ukuba abantu beze kwiiloki shi zethu babone ukuba siphi la impilo yesiXhosa endilise kileyo kwaye sifuna nabanye abantu baze kuzibonela ngo kwabo," wongeze watsho uNo mzamo.
Njengoko iyiNyanga ye -zoKhenketho, eli qela sele libambe imisitho emibini eyinxalenye yamalungise lelo, entenda futhi ephekela amalungu ecandelo lezokhe nketho ebetyelele apha.
"Siwenze onke amalungise lelo kwaye sivuya kakhulu kuba siza kutyelelwa ngaba khenkethi bangaphandle njengeendwendwe zethu. Si nethemba lokuba siza kufu mana ithuba lokubona indlela eliqhuba nelisebenza ngayo icandelo lezokhenketho. Lo msitho uza kubonisa nokuba kuninzi okunokonwatyelwa futhi kwenziwe ngabakhe nkethi beli nabangapha ndle kwiilokishi zoMza ntsi Afrika," uqhube watsho uNomzamo.
UMlawuli we-George Tou rism uJoan Shaw ucacise wathi kule nkqubo kuza kuboniswa iintlobo ezahlu keneyo zenkcubeko zeli.
"Inkqubo yethu ye-Dine with a Locals ingumzeke lo omhle obonisa iintla nga ezahlukeneyo zeli, apho kuza kuphekwa kungcanyu lwe iintlobo zokutya ezahlu keneyo, eziquka ukutya kwabantu abangamaXhosa, abangamaVenda, abanga maKhoi, indlela ekuphekwa ngayo yi-Cape Malay kunye nokutya okubizwa i-'Snoek and Patat," ucacise watsho uShaw.
Ekupheleni kwalo msitho, la maqela aza kuba namashi shini agcweleyo asebenzisana ne-George Tourism, yona eza kuthi ibhengeze futhi isebe nzise iinkonzo nezinto ezithe ngiswa ngala maqela.
