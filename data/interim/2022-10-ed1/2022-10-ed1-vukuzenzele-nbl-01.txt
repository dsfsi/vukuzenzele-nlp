USewula 2022 umGadangiso 1Page 7 Page 6Grandchicks thriving in poultry Vuk’uzenzeleUkuvuselelwa Kwe-N3 Kuzuzisa AmaSewula Owen Mngadi UMduduzi Jijana umnikazi weHlangano eThuthukisa abaNtu abaKhubazekileko, engenye yamakhampani azuzako sikhuluma nje ngemali emaBhiliyoni ama-R26 ngokuvuselelwa kukathelawalisa wendlela i-N3 ehlangana ne-Pietermaritzburg ne-Durban eKwaZulu-Natala.Iphrojekthi le, eyenganyelwe yi-Ejensi yokwAkhiwa kweeNdlela yeliZweloke (i-Sanral SOC Limited), iqinisekisa ukuthi abosomabhizinisi  abanjengoJijana abazuzi kwaphela kodwana bavulela nabanye amathuba wemise-benzi. UJijana, onokukhubazeka, uthi ikhampani yakhe ifakela amatshwayo weendlela phakathi kwe-Hilton ne-Pietermaritzburg. Uqatjhe abantu abali-13 godu uthi iphrojekthi le itjhugulule namaphilo wabo. “Njengabantu abanokukhubazeka, sitjengisile ukuthi siyakghona. Asisathembeli kusibonelelo sezehlalakuhle kwaphela. Senza umsebenzi wamambala,” kwatjho uJijana.UmPhathi weHlelo i-Sanral N3 u-Tsepo Matekane uthi ikhampani yakhe iqinisekise ukuthi ama-30% womsebenzi wabelwe amakhampani wendawo anekghono lokwenza imisebenzi ethileko. Lokhu kwavulela amabhizinisi amancani welutjha amathuba amanengi, newabantu abadinywa amathuba ngaphambilini newabomma. “Kunamabhizinisi ali-108 angamancani, aphakathi namakhulu asebenzako njenganje kumaphrojekthi ahlukahlukeneko,” ngokutjho kwakhe.UNokuthula Zondi we-Ingqalabutho Yamalinda Multipurpose Trading wanikelwa ikontraka yokuletha iqulwani nokufakela amapha yiphi wamanzi. Uthi ikhampani yakhe yaqatjha abasebenzi abane kghono ababunane, ekubalwa hlangana nabo abatjhayeli. UZondi ungezelele ngokuthi abomma banekghono njengamadoda godu ikhampani yakhe izenzele igama emsebenzini lo. Wangezelela ngokuthi, “Sizwisisa ukuqakatheka kwesikhathi ngakho siyaphumelela ukuqeda umsebenzi ngeenkhathi esibekelwe zona ukuqinisekisa ukuthi iphrojekthi ikhamba ngefanelo. Sithabela ithubeli godu sithemba ukuthi kusazokuvela amanye ukuze ikhampani yethu ikhule ukuya ngokwamazinga wezokwakha”.
Amaphrojekthi Karhulumende Aqakathekileko Welizweloke U-Matekane uthi ukuthuthukiswa kwe-Durban-Free State-Gauteng Logistics and Industrial Corridor kubekelwe isabelomali esilinganiselwa kumaBhiliyoni ama-R26 begodu ingenye yamaphrojekthi karhulumende aqakathekileko welizweloke nahlanganisiweko.
