Kusutsa kuhambisana nekutfola imiphumela lemihle

More Matshediso

Bantfwana labange- tulu kwetigidzi leti- yimfica kulolonkhe
laseNingizimu Afrika laba- setikolweni tahulumende sebayakhona kwenta umse- benti wabo kancono, sibo- nga loluhlelo lwahulumende lolucinisekisa kwekutsi tisu tabo tihlale tisemfi ngekudla lokunemsoco.
Litiko Letemfundvo Lesise-
kelo (i-DBE), ngekusebentisa Luhlelo Lwavelonkhe Lwe- tikolo Lwekondla (i-NSNP), liyengcile injongo yalo yekunika kudla bantfwana bakuleli labalinganiselwa kumaphesenti lange-75.

Umcondzisi we-NSNP kule- litiko, Neo Rakwena, utsite tikolo leti-20 619 tiphekela tibuye tinike kudla etiko-lweni onkhe malanga ba- ntfwana labalinganiselwa eti-9 032 622 velonkhe.
Utsite tikolo letingasiyo ince- nye yaloluhlelo kufanele kutsi tivakashele emahhovisi asesigodzini sabo uma ngabe tifuna kuba yincenye yalo. N gemnyakatimali	wa- nga-2015/16, tigidzi letili- nganiselwa ku-R5 703 tafa- kwa kuloluhlelo.
Rakwena  utsite  lemali 
lecitfwe kuloluhlelo iyachi- tjiyelwa njalo ngemnyaka kute ihambisane nekunyuka kwetintfo, kantsi ibhajethi yanga-2017/18 ilinganiselwa kutigidzi leti-R6.4.
Loluhlelo lucedza kulamba kwesikhashana kantsi futsi lufaka sandla kunhlalakahle yebantfwana.
“Bantfwana labanyenti ba- vela emakhaya lahluphekile langakhoni kuba nekudla labangakudla, kantsi ku-
labanye bafundzi, lokudla
labakudla esikolweni ngiko kudla labakudlela lilanga lonkhe. Kudla labakudla esi- kolweni kuyabasita kutsi bakhone kufundza, kulale- lisisa nekutimbandzakanya eklasini,” washo njalo.
Emanani ebafundzi lababha- lisako etikolweni nalabange- na sikolo anyukile ngenca yaloluhlelo lwekutfola kudla esikolweni.
Kungasikudzala, iKhabhi- nethi ivume kushicilelwa kwe Mbiko Wekulingani- swakwe-NSNP lobhalwe Litiko Letekuhlela, Kuca- phela kanye Nekulungisa (i-DPME). Uchaza imphume- lelo netinyatselo letingatsa- tfwa kuphumelelisa lemita- mo.
Umcondzisi Wekulinganisa
ka-DPME, Jabu Mathe, utsite injongo lenkhulu yekuli- nganisa kwekutsi kuhlolwe kutsi ngabe i-NSNP yenti- wa ngalendlela letawuba nemiphumela lekhombisa umehluko lomkhulu kutem- philo kantsi kube nenzuzo emfundvweni yebafundzi. Uphindze wagcizelela kwe- kutsi injongo yaloluhlelo seyiphelele kwekutsi kwenti- we kancono temphilo nesimo sekudla kahle kwebafundzi labaphuye kakhulu eNingi- zimu Afrika. Loluhlelo lu- hlose kufinyelela kubo bonkhe  bantfwana  laba-
setikolweni tahulumende
letisemkhakheni welizi- nga lebuphuya 1 kuya ku-3, letilinganiselwa kumaphe- senti la-60 etikolweni leti- phuye kakhulu eNingizimu Afrika.
Loluhlelo luphindze futsi lusite ngekwehlisa buphuya emimangweni ngekwakha ematfuba emsebenti.
“ Lokulinganisa kutfole kwekutsi i-NSNP inika ematfuba kulabatinikele kusebenta ngalokudla la- bangetulu kweti-50 000 njalo ngemnyaka lekufanele kutsi baphekele bantfwana, kantsi batfola imali yekubabonga lenge-R960 njalo ngenya- nga,” kusho Mathe.
“Loku kusho kutsi tigidzi
letinge-R576 ngemnyaka ti- yiswa kumholo wekubabo- nga kute kuzuze emalunga emmango.”
Loluhlelo lukhutsata ku- tsi kubekhona lokwentiwa kutemnotfo njengoba boso- mabhizinisi labasebancane, labasemkhatsini nalese- bakhulile kanye netinhjlanga- no letisebenta ngekuhlanga- nyela tiyazuza njengebantfu labatfula tinsita kuleto ti- fundza ngekutsi umsebe- nti uniketwe ngekulingana (KaZulu-Natal, eGauteng, eMpumalanga naseNyaka- tfo Kapa).
