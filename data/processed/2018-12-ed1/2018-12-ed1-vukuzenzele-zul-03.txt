Sebenzisa imali ngobuhlakani 

Allison Cooper

Amaholide kaKhisi musi akufanele aku shiye unembombo yomgodi wezikweleti uma ephela.
“Ukusebenzisa imali nge ndlela engafanele kufana nesikweleti esiyiphela e ndlebeni. Kuhle ukugwema ukuchitha imali ngendlela engafanele kulawa maholide kaKhisimusi ngoba kungaba nomthelela omkhulu emalini yakho kanye nakuhlelo lwakho esikhathini esizayo,” ngokusho komphathi wezokukhangisa nokudayisa e-DebtSafe u-Matthys Po tgieter.
“Ubumnandi bokuthenga ngenyanga kaLwezi noZi -bandlela bobesikhashana futhi abathengi bazokuzwa ubunzima bokusaphaza ima li ekupheleni koMasingana 2019. Ungayisebenzisi imali ongenayo,” kwengeza u-Po tgieter.
U-Mellony Ramalho we-A frican Bank uthi abasebenzi bajwayele ukusheshe bahole ngenyanga kaZibandlela, okwenza bawulinde eside isikhathi umholo wabo wa ngoMasingana.
“UMasingana inyanga enzi ma kubantu abaningi, abanye basebenzisa amakhadi ezi kweleti noma baboleke ima li. Kubalulekile ukuqaphela ukuthi imali yakho uyisebenzi sa kanjani. Okugcono enza uhlu lwezinto ofuna ukuyi sebenzisa kuzo imali yakho ukuze ugweme ukusebenzisa imali ngendlela engafanele,” ngokusho kuka-Ramalho.
U-Potgieter ululeke aba thengi ukuba baqale uku thenga izinto zikaKhisimusi kusenesikhathi futhi baqikelele ukuthi bathenga ukudla okwanele futhi bathenge izi nhlobo zokudla ezizohlala isikhathi eside, ukuze banga sebenzisi imali ngendlela engafanele futhi bagweme nokuthenga izinto ezibizayo ngemizuzu yokugcina.  Le indlela u-Potgieter aluleka ngayo abathengi ukuze bagcine imali yabo isesimweni esihle.
•	Thenga ukudla okwanele futhi okuyizinhlobo ezi-zohlala isikhathi eside•	Cabanga ngokusebenzisa imoto eyodwa. Ukusebe nzisa izimoto ezimbalwa kuchaza ukusebenzisa imali encane kuphethiloli.
•	Gwema imigwaqo ekho khelwayo uma kungenze-ka.
•	Esikhundleni sokuthenga izipho vumela abantwana ukuba babhake izinto ezi-hlukile babhakele izihlobo.
•	Qala uthenge izinto zika Khisimusi kusenesikhathi.
Kulo nyaka u-DebtSafe ukhu thaza abathengi ukuba bazame ukusebenzisa amaphepha ajwayelekile ansundu uma besonga izipho, ashibhile futhi ahambisana nemvelo.
