Sivulela Ilutjha le-KZN Amathuba Wemisebenzi

Hlengiwe Ngobese

Ilutjha elibalelwa ema20 laKwaZulu-Nata la libekelwe ukuzuza ekwakhiweni kweFemu  etja-tja yakwa-Mahindra ngaphandlenyana kwe-Dur ban, ebize iingidi ezima-95 zamaranda ezokukhiqiza umhlobo omutjha weemve ni ezinamabadi amane (ama-double cab) ezizokuthiywa bona ma-Pik Up.
Abantu abatjha abama-25 baKwaZulu-Natala  bafunyene ibandulo bebathola imisebenzi ngoku vulwa kwebubulo elitjha lakwa-Mahindra.
Abasebenzi babandulwe ngokwecwephetjhe lamazwe ngamazwe yi-Mahin dra Group isebenzisana ne-AIH Logistics. Nakunesidingo bazokuthunyelwa e-India khona bazakufu nyana ibandulo elidepha dephileko.
Umsebenzi wokukhiqiza sewuthomile nanyana  usalingelelwa nje begodu kubekwe isilinganiso  sokukhiqiza iinkoloyi  ezizii-2000 ngomnyaka, ngokukhamba kwesikhathi inani leenkoloyi ezikhiqizwako lizakuba zii-4000 ngomnyaka.
“Ukusisokhu kuzakwenza bona amanye amabubulo abe nethemba phezu ko mnotho welizwe lekhethu. Lokhu kuyikomba yokobana isifunda saKwaZu lu-Natala siyindawo ehle nenothileko kezokusisa.
Lokho kuthumela umlayezo kilabo abanganathemba kilesisifunda ukobana bazi bonyana i-Durban yindawo engasetjenziswa kezamasiso,’’ kwatjho uSomkhandlu wezokuThuthukiswa komNotho, zokuVakatjha neze Bhoduluko uSihle Zikalala lokha nakuvulwa ifemu le.
Uveza bona amathuba womsebenzi abawuthomi leko erhwebeni azokuletha ukukhula kezamakghono esifundeni.
Omunye wabasebenzi  ongusonjiniyera ngokwe ziqu zakhe, nguSthembiso Mthembu othi uthabe khulu ngokufunyana umsebenzi lo.
 ‘’Bengihlezi ekhaya pheze umnyaka woke ngingatho li umsebenzi. Ithubeli lingisize khulu bonyana ngikghone ukuzondla nebomndenami. Ngineziqu zobunjiniyera bokulungisa kodwana akhange ngithole ithuba lokukhanda na mkha lokwakha ikoloyi. Ibandulo abasinikele lona lisisize khulu ukobana sibe nokuzithemba nelemuko lomsebenzi,’’ ubeke watjho.
IsiKhulu esiLawulako semiSebenzi yeenTjhaba tjhaba yakwa-Mahindra, u-Arvind Matthew uveza bona ikampani inqophe ukuthenga ama-40% wee nsimbi ze-Pik Up kubakhi qizi beSewula Afrika. ‘’Ukukhiqizwa kwe-Pik Up eSewula Afrika kuliga dango lokuthoma lamahlelo wethu wesikhathi eside, lawo azakungezelela ama thuba wemisebenzi esi -fundeni kunye namathuba wokuthengwa kweensimbi nezinye iinsetjenziswa zokukhanda namkha zo kwenza iinkoloyi KwaZu lu-Natala,’’ ubeke watjho .
