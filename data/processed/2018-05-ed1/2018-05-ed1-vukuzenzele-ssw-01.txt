Lusha luyalwemukela luhlelo lwe-YES

UMTAMO WAHULUMENDE lohlose kuniketa bantfu labasha labasigidzi labangasebenti lwati lwemsebenti babuye babakhokhele.

Masego Temane  uyatfokota ku tsi atfole lwati lwemsebenti, lolutawuha mbisana neticu takhe teDi ploma yeHuman Resource Management, njengalongene lele Kuletinsita Tekucashwa Kwelusha (i-YES).
YES, lesandza kuvulwa ngu Mengameli Cyril Ramapho sa, ihlose kulungiselela lusha kutsi batfole umsebenti iba nikete emakhono ebuchwe pheshe ladzingekako kute kuciniswe ku tfutfukiswa kwemno tfo. Temane uyachaza kweku tsi ngemuva kwekutfola ticu nga-2016 akazange akhone kufaka ticelo temsebenti ngoba bekangenalo lwati lwemsebenti loludzingeka ko.
“Bekulukhuni kufuna umse benti ngaphandle kwekuba nelwati lwemsebenti. Nge nhlahla ngahlangabetana ne Lulaway Recruitment Age ncy lesebentisana ne-YES, kantsi banginiketa litfuba lokufundzela umsebenti e msebentini, i-internship.”I-YES ingumtamo loyinhla nganisela wahulumende, ti nhlangano tetisebenti nema bhizinisi kutsi banikete ba ntu labasha baseNingizimu Afrika labasigidzi misebenti lekhokhelwayo kuleminyaka lemitsatfu letako.
Lucwaningo lolwenti we lukhombisa kutsi lwati lwemsebenti lwemnyaka munye lolubhala ku-CV , nencwadzi yereferensi, ku tfutfukisa ematfuba emuntfu lomusha ekutfola umsebenti emahlandla lamatsatfu.
Temane ungete ngekutsi uyawutsandza umsebenti wakhe, ikakhulukati ngoba usita lolunye lusha kutfo la imisebenti njengoba ayi intern lecashako.
Ngalesikhatsi kuvulwa i-YES Mengameli Rama phosa watiswa kulabantfu  labasha bekucala laba-100  labatawube bacashwe yi ABSA, Investec, Netcare, Sasol ne-Unilever ngaphansi kwa loluhlelo.
“Lomtamo siwubona  njengesisekelo sinye save lonkhe lesikhulu nalesifa ka ekhatsi konkhe lesizama kwakha ematfuba elusha.
Lesinye sisekelo lesise mkhatsini ngulokutfutfu kiswa kwalamakhono nelwa ti lwelusha,” kusho Menga meli Ramaphosa.
Ucele bosomabhizinisi la basebancane kutsi badlale indzima yabo njengoba bane tikhala letinyenti temsebenti washo nekutsi emabhizinisi lamakhulu angeke akhona kutsatsa lusha lolwanele awodvwa.
Ngekusho kwa-CEO weYES Tashmia Ismail-Saville, kubantfu labatigidzi leti ngu-15.5 eNingizimu Afrika labaneminyaka yebudzala lesemkhatsini we-18 ne-34, tigidzi letingu-5.8 abase benti.
“I-YES ihlose kuniketa leyo ncenye yelusha lwetfu, leba nganakwa nguletindlela le kucashwa ngato kwanyalo kulelive, litfuba lekucala lekutfola umsebenti losezi ngeni lelisetulu nalokahle kanye nelwati lwemsebenti babe bakhokhelwa,” kusho Ismail-Saville.
Njengenzuzo lengetiwe kumabhizinisi lasemanca ne, labo labacasha bantfu labasha labamnyama, la baneminyaka yebudzala lesemkhatsini we-18 ne-29, batawulungela kutsi bazuze Sibonelelo Semtselo Weku casha lesilingana na-R1 000 ngenyanga.
Lusha lwe-YES kufanele:
• Lube neminyaka le-18 ne-34 budzala.
• Bangasebenti sikhatsi  lesindlula tinyanga letisitfupha.
• Kube ngulabamnyama (labamnyama bomdza bu, Emakhaladi noma Emandiya).
• Uma kwenteka tinka pani tifisa kutfola Sibo nelelo Semtselo Weku casha, lolusha kufanele lube neminyaka lese mkhatsini we-18 ne-29.
Lolusha lutawuhola malini?Lomholo ulindzeleke kutsi ubekwe ngekusebentisa sili nganiso semholo lolizinga ncane wavelonkhe lesingu R3 500  ngenyaga, lokufaka ekha tsi kuceceshwa nekusekelwa lokuhambisanako. Silinganiso lesetayelekile sibita R55 000 ngemnyaka.

Kunelulwimi lolusemtsetfweni lolusha loluhlongotwako eNingizimu Afrika

BAHOLI BENDZABUKO banendzima lebalulekile lekufanele bayidlale ekwakheni ummango longenabuphuya, longenako kuswelakala kwemsebenti nalonekulingana.

Neo Semono

I-Khelobedu ingahle  ibe lulwimi loluse mtsetfweni lwema-12 eNingizimu Afrika ngenca yekuhambisana nekubuyi swa kwesitfunti sebaholi bendzabuko.
Mengameli Cyril Rama phosa wente lesimeme telo ngalesikhatsi semci mbi wekubeka Indlovukati  yeBalobedu eMokwakwa ila Stadium eBolobedu,  Ga-Modjadji eLimpopo.
I-Khelobedu lulwimi  lolukhulunywa sive seBa lobedu lapho khona, Masala nabo Modjadji oyiNdlovuka ti yeLitulu lengakabekwa leneminyaka leli-13 budzala, atawutsatsa lesihlalo nakaba neminyaka le-18, ngemuva kwekutsi abekwe njenge Ndlovukazi Modjadji VII.Utawuphotfula ngekuha mbisana nemihambo  nemasiko esive seBalobedu.
“Loku yincophamlandvo Mama Winnie Madikize la-Mandela bekatatigcabha ngayo. Lesi sihlalo sekuca la futsi ngiso sodvwa seBu ndlovukati kulelive nakule livekati,” kusho Mengameli Ramaphosa.
Mengameli Ramapho sa ubuye wacinisekisa ku tinikela kwahulumende  ekubuyiseleni kuhlonipha nesitfunti sebuholi bendza buko.
Bukhosi besive seBalobedu bamukelwe ngalokusemtse tfweni ngenyanga yeNdlovu lenkhulu nga-2016 ngemuva kwekutsi i-Commission on Traditional Leadership Di sputes and Claims (i-CT LDC) yenta lucwaningo ngesimo sesive seBalobedu, ngekusho kwaticelo letile tfwe ku-CTLDC.
“Hulumende utinikele  ekuphumeleliseni letigaba temtsetfo teSahluko se-12 kuMtsetfosisekelo, ikakhu lukati lokubuyiswa kwesi tfunti salesikhungo sebuho li bendzabuko embusweni wentsandvo yelunyenti loho lwa ngeMtsetfosisekelo,” ku sho Mengameli Ramaphosa.
Utsite kuvunyelwa ko muntfu losikati kutsi abekwe njengeNdlovukati  eNingi zimu Afrika kukhombi sa kutinikela kulomgomo wekulingana ngekwebulili, ngalesikhatsi kubuye kuna kwe lendzima yebuholi ledla lwa bomake kuyo yonkhe imi khakha yemphilo.
“Baholi bendzabuko  banendzima lebalulekile le kufanele bayidlale ekwa kheni ummango longenabu phuya, longenako kuswe lakala kwemsebenti na lonekulingana. Baholi betfu bendzabuko kudzingeka ku tsi bahambe phambili eku lweni nekubuyisela emalu ngelo nesitfunti sebantfu ba kitsi.” Uchubeke wagcizelela  lokubaluleka kwalendzima ledlalwe baholi bendzabuko ekubuyiseni umhlaba kubani kati lebafanele.
“Badzinga kucinisekisa kwekutsi umhlaba ubuyise lwa kulabo labawusebe ntako kantsi labo labatawu niketwa umhlaba kufanele babe netindlela tekuwusebe nta,” kusho Mengameli Rama phosa.
Bantfu labalinganiselwa ku tigidzi leti-25 labahlala ema phandleni bakwenta loku ngaphansi kwebuholi beba holi bendzabuko.
