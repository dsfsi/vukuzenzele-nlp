I-MISA Sisekelo Sepumelelo Yomplanimadorobha

IhlElO lIKArhuluMENDE alisizi kwaphela ngokuqinisekisa ukubandulwa ngefanelo kwaborhulumende bekhaya kodwana litjhugulula namaphilo wabantu abatjha.

More Matshediso

ukukhulela ekhaya elalinganabuyo  begodu liphethwe ngumma ombelethi otjheja abentwana ayedwa kwaku ngaqintelisa amathuba  wetuthuko yomntwana  kodwana lokho akhange kukhandele u-Vele Mu khodiwa ekuphumeleliseni ibhudango lakhe lokuba mplanimadorobha.
U-Mukhodiwa, obuya esabelweni se-Luheni ngapha si koMasipala weNdawo i-Thulamela eseLimpopo, wafumana iZiqu ze-Urban and Regional Planning eYunivesithi ye-Venda ngomnya ka wee-2016.
Ungomunye wabazu zi ehlelweni lokuSeke lwa kweFundo yeLutjha ngaphasi kw-Ejensi eSekela umThangalasisekelo kaMasipala (i-MISA). Ihle lweli linqophe ukunikela abafundi abaneziqu ithuba lokufumana ilemuko lomsebenzi emikhakheni ebuthekhnikhali beba kghone ukutloliswa ee nhlanganweni zabasebenzi bamabizelo.
I-MISA iliphiko lika rhulumende welizweloke ngaphasi komNyango wezokuBusa ngokuBambisana. Umkhakha lo wahlonywa ngoMrhayili wee-2012.
“Ngemva kokuphasa uMethrigi ngomnyaka wee-2008, ngahlala umnyaka woke ngi cabanga bonyana ngizoku bhadela ngani iindle ko zeyunivesithi,”kwatjho u-Mukhodiwa.
Ngomnyaka wee-2010, wa tlolisa eYunivesithi ye Jwanasbhege, ngetjhudu elimbi ukutlhayela kweemali khange kumvumele uko bana aqede ngitjho nee mfundo zomnyaka wakhe wokuthoma, ngenca yalo kho walisa.
Ngomnyaka olandelako, watlolisela iimfundo ze-Ele ctrical Engineering N3 e kholiji yangekhabo ko dwana wabona bonyana leziimfundo azikhambe-lani nebhudango lakhe. Wathoma phasi godu,  wathumela iimbawo zokwa mukelwa emayunivesithi ngethemba lokufumana isizo leemali zokufunda.
Ngetjhudu, iYunivesithi ye-Venda yamukela isibawo sakhe  wabe wathoma ukufundela iziqu ze-Urban and Regional Planning ngomnyaka wee-2012.
Wathi, “isiKhwama seli Zweloke seSizo leeMali zokuFunda (i-NSFAS) sa bhadela iindleko zokufu nda zomnyaka wami wo kuthoma, i-MISA yange na ngomfundalize nasele emnyakeni wesibili yangi sekela bengaqeda iimfu ndo zami ngomnyaka wee-2016”.
Umfundalize lo wabhadela zoke iindleko zakhe, ezifaka hlangana iimali zokufunda, ukuthengwa kweencwadi, i ndawo yokuhlala nokugoma. Ngomnyaka wee-2017 u-Mukhodiwa wathoma iba ndulolemuko lomsebe nzi kwaMasipala weNdawo i-Prince Albert ngaphasi kwezemiSebenzi yomNya ngo neZenzelwa zomPha kathi.
“Lihlala likhona ithuba lokufikelela okufunako epi lweni, indabakulu kubekeze la. Kufanele wenze koke oku semandlenakho ukobana utjhugulule ubujamo ba kho bewuthuthukise ipilwa kho,” kwatjho u-Mukhodi wa.
Ihlelo lokuSekelwa kwe Fundo yeLutjha ngapha si kwe-Ejensi eSekela  umThangalasisekelo kaMasipala njenganje linabafu ndi abama-70 esele bathwe swe iziqu, hlangana nabo abaplani bemakho yeeyingi neyamadorobha bama-31, abosonjiniyera begezi bama24, abali-12 ngabaneziqu ze-Civil Engineering kuthi bathathu kube baphathi kezokwakha nakumaphroje kthi. Boke baqatjhwe boma sipala eemfundeni zolithoba njengengcenye yalelihlelo.
UmJaphethe womPhathi oyiHloko kwa-MISA  uNtandazo Vimba uthe  i-ofisakhe izwisisa isidi ngo esiqakathekileko  samakghonofundwa wa bosonjiniyera newezazi ke zokwakha eSewula Afrika.
UVimba uthe, “Asifuni kwaphela abantu abatjha abaneziqu kilemikhakha, kodwana kufanele siqinise kise bonyana baneba ndulo elamukelekako  ngokwezinga leentjhaba tjhaba begodu batloli swe neenhlangano eze ngamele ibizelo labo”.
Wangezelela ngokuthi, “Abomasipala bethu batlho ga khulu abantu abafundele amakghono. Sinomsebenzi omkhulu wokutjhugulula urhulumende weendawo zemakhaya ukuthi asebenze ngefanelo ngemizamo yethu yokuthuthukisa amakgho no”. 


Ababelethi Bazokubonakala Emakhethweni We-SGB

ABABElEthI BANEthuBA lokubamba indima ebonakalako ekulawulweni kweenkolo ngomnqopho wokuthuthukisa amazinga nokwenza iinkolo zibe yindawo engcono yabentwana.


Amakhetho we miKhandlu e Lawula iinKolo (ama-SGB) azokubanjwa ngoNtaka nonyaka, ka nti ababelethi bayakhu thazwa ukuzibandaka nya khona bazokufaka isandla ngokubonakala ko efundweni yabentaba bo.
Amakhetho azokuba njwa kizo zoke iinkolo zombuso elizweni mazombe.
UNgqongqotjhe weze Fundo esiSekelo u-Angie Motshekga uthe ama khetho la angamakhu lu ngelizweni kunga sabalwa amakhetho  waborhulumende bemakhaya newelizwe  loke.
“Amakhetho wama-SGB asiza ngokungezelela amazizo womphakathi ekubeni nelizwi eku-lawulweni kweenkolo. Ukusebenza kweenkolo kuyathuthuka lokha ababelethi bafaka isandla  ngokubonakalako ee ndabeni zesikolo.” Uthe amabhodi alawula iinkolo anendima ekulu ekulinganiseni amakareko wababambindima ngomnqopho wokusekela inembombono efanako yokufikelela imiphumela yefundo emihle nekhambi sana needingo zompha kathi nokuthuthukiswa kezomnotho.
Ama-SGB ajamele yoke imikhakha yomphakathi ozombeleze iinkolo, kufaka hlangana ababelethi, abotitjhere, abasebenzi besikolweni abangasibo abotitjhere nabafundi.
Isikhathi se-ofisi sokuphatha kwamalunga weSGB minyaka emithathu.Igunya lokulawula iinkolo UmThetho oLawula  iinKolo weSewula Afrika unikela ama-SGB amagu nya aneleko wokulawula iinkolo. Lokhu kukha mbisana nokuhloni tjhwa okuqakathekileko kwamaziko asekela inta ndo yenengi.Ngokuzibandaka nya emakhethweni wa ma-SGB, ababelethi  baqinisekisa bonyana iindingo zabentababo eenkolweni ziyatjhejwa.
Nanyana kungadingeki bonyana ababelethi abafu na ukuba malunga we-SGB babe neziqu zefundo, ababelethi abanamakghono wokulondwa kwemi niningwana, ukuhlolwa kweencwadi zeemali ne lwazi lezomthetho  abanekareko, begodu banetjisakalo ekuthuthukisweni kweze fundo, bayakhuthazwa ukobana bangenele ama khetho njengabongikhe thani.
