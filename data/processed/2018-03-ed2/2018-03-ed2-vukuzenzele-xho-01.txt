I-MISA yanceda ingcali yezokwakhiwa kweedolophi

More Matshediso

ukukhulela kwikhaya elihlelelekileyo ne likhokelwa ngu mama ongatshatanga onga phangeliyo kungakwenza uzidele futhi uzoyikisele kwezinye izinto  kodwa oku zange kumthintele uVele Mukhodiwa ekuleqeni  iphupha lakhe lokuba yingca li yokwakhiwa kweedolo phu.  UMukhodiwa, ovela kwi lali yaseLuheni kuMasi pala weNgingqi  yaseThulamela eLimpo po, wafumana imfu ndo yesiDanga kwi Urban and Regional Planni ng kwiYunivesithi yaseVenda ngowama-2016.
Ungomnye wabantu aba xhamla kwi-Arhente yeNkxa so kaMasipala yeziSeko zezi Bonelelo (i-MISA) yeNkqubo yeziThwala-zidanga eziTsha. I-MISA yinkqubo ezamela izi thwala-zidanga amathu ba emisebenzi nawokufu mana uqeqesho ngomse benzi usemsebenzi ni kwiinkalo ezifunekayo zobugcisa ukuze zibhaliswe njengeengcali.
I-MISA licandelo lika rhulumente kazwelonke eliyinxenye yeSebe loLawulo lweNtsebe nziswano. Yasekwa ngekaCa nzibe ngowama-2012.
“Emva kokuphumelela iba nga le-12 ngowama-2008, nda nyanzeleka ukuba ndibe nonyaka endingabhali swanga ndawo kuwo ukuze ndicebe indlela endiza kuyi hlawula ngayo iimali zase yunivesithi,” watsho uMu khodiwa.
Ngowama-2010, wabhalisa kwiYunivesithi yaseRhawu tini kodwa ingxaki yema li zange imvumele ukuba awugqibe unyaka wokuqala wakhe waze wanyanzeleka ukuba asiyeke isikolo.
Kunyaka olandelayo, wabhalisela izifu ndo zeN3 kwi-Ele-ctrical Engineering kwikholeji ya lapha kodwa wakhawuleza waqonda ukuba ezo zifundo azilobizo lwakhe. Waphinda waqalisa ukuthumela izicelo kwiiyunivesithi ngethemba lokufumana inkxaso-mali yokufunda.
Ngethamsanqa, iYunivesithi yaseVenda yasamkela isicelo sakhe waze waqalisa ngesiDanga kwi-Urban and Regional Planning  ngowama-2012.
“IsiKimu sikaZwelonke so Ncedo lwabaFundi nge Mali yokuFunda (i-NSFAS) sandihlawulela izifundo zam zonyaka wokuqala yaza i-MISA yandinika ibhasari xa bendisenza u nyaka wam wesibini ya ndixhasa ngolo hlobo ndade ndazigqiba izifundo zam ngowama-2016,” watsho.
Ibhasari yahlawula zonke iindleko zakhe zemfundo, kuquka ukuhlohlwa, iincwadi, indawo yokuhlala noku tya.
Ngo-2017 uMukhodi wa waqalisa ngengqesho yoqhelaniso nomsebe nzi kuMasipala weNgingqi wasePrince Albert kwiSebe leeNkonzo zemiSebe nzi yeSebe nezoLuntu.
“Akunakuze ushiywe lixe sha ekufikeni apho ufuna ukuba khona ebomini. Kufuneka wenze konke okuse mandleni akho ukuguqula iimeko okuzo ngokunjalo uphucule iimeko ophila kuzo,” utshilo uMukho diwa.
Inkqubo yeziThwala-zida nga eziTsha i-MI SA ngoku inezithwa la-zidanga ezingama-70, kuquka iingcali zokucwa ngcisa nokwakhiwa kweedo lophu neengingqi ezinga ma-31, iinjineli zombane ezi ngama-24, iinjineli zezokwa kha ezili-12 kunye nee ngcali zezokwakha na baphathi beeprojekthi  abathathu. Bonke bakoo masipala kuwo onke amapho ndo alithoba njengenxenye yenkqubo.
Intloko yesiGqeba esiLa wulayo (i-CEO) eliBambela ye-MISA uNtandazo Vimba uthe i-ofisi yakhe iyakuqo-nda ukuba kukho ingxaki enkulu yokunqongophala kweengcali ezinezakhono zobunjineli nezokwakha eMzantsi Afrika.
“Asifuni kuphela abantu abatsha abaneziqiniseki so zemfundo kwezi  zifundo, kodwa sifuna  ukuqinisekisa ukuba bafumana uqeqesho oluku mgangatho wehlabathi ji kelele kwaye bazingcali ezi bhalisiweyo,” watsho uVi mba.
“Oomasipala banengxa ki enkulu yokunqo ngaphala kweengca li ezinezakhono eziku mgangatho ophezulu.  Sinoxanduva olukhu lu lokuqinisekisa ukuba oomasipala banabasebe nzi abazingcali ezineza khono ezikumgangatho  ophezulu, ngokusebenzi sa amalinge ethu oku xhobisa abasebenzi ngeza khono,” uqokele watsho.


Abazali baza kuzibalula kunyulo lwee-SGB

ABAZAlI BANEthuBA lokudlala indima ephambili kulawulo lwezikolo zikarhulumente ngenjongo yokuphucula umgangatho wokusebenza nokwenza isikolo sibe yindawo ephucukileyo yabantwana babo.

unyulo lwamaqu mrhu olawula  lwesikolo (i-SGB) luza kubanjwa ngeyo Kwindla kwaye abaza li bayathundezwa ukuba bathathe inxaxheba ukuze babandakanyeke kwimfu ndo yabantwana babo.
Unyulo luza kwenziwa kuzo zonke izikolo zika rhulumente kulo lonke eli lizwe.
UMphathiswa weMfu ndo esiSiseko u-Angie  Motshekga uthe olu lelona nyulo lukhulu kweli lizwe, ngaphandle konyulo luka rhulumente weengingqi (oo masipala) nolukazwelonke.
“Unyulo lwee-SGB lwe nza ukuba uluntu nabahla li bazive beyinxalenye fu thi benesabelo kwimfundo yabantwana. Umgangatho wokusebenza wezikolo  ukholisa ukuphucuka xa abazali bezibandakanya ngenkuthalo nangomdla kwimicimbi yesikolo.”Uthe ii-SGB zinendima enkulu ezifanele ukuyi dlala ukuqinisekisa uku ba zonke izinto ezibalule kileyo neziphambili kubo bonke abachaphazelekayo zithathelwa ingqalelo ukuze kulwelwe ukuzalisekisa imibono yabo yeziphumo ezincumisayo zemfundo ezihambelana ngqo nee mfuno zoluntu nophuhliso loqoqosho.
Ii-SGB zimele bonke aba ntu abachaphazelekayo na badlala indima. Aba bantu baquka abazali, ootitshala, abantu abancedisa esiko lweni kunye nabafundi. Ixesha elihlalwa ngamalu ngu e-SGB yiminyaka emi thathu.Amagunya okulawula izikoloUmthetho weziKolo zase Mzantsi Afrika unika ii-SGB amagunya abalulekileyo okulawula izikolo njengenxa lenye yamaziko abalulekileyo olawulo ngentando yoninzi.Ngokuzibandakanya  kwinkqubo yonyulo, abazali baqinisekisa ukuba isikolo sisibenzela futhi sinceda abantwana babo.Nangona kungeyomfuneko ukuba amalungu e-SGB abe neziqinisekiso zemfundo yase-sikolweni, abazali abaneza khono zobuchule bokugcina iincwadi, ezocwangciso-mali kunye nezomthetho ngoku njalo abo banomdla nabano thando olushushu emfu ndweni, bayakhuthazwa uku ba bazigqatse kunyulo.

Aba bathathi-nxaxheba balandelayo banokunyulwa kwi-SGB:
• Abazali babantwana abakweso sikolo.
• Ootitshala abakweso sikolo.
• Abasebenzi kweso sikolo abangengobahlohli.
• Abafundi besikolo samabanga aphezulu.
Yintoni imisebenzi ye-SGB?
• Umgaqo-nkqubo wokwamkelwa kwabafundi esikolweni.
• Ukwenza izigqibo ngomgaqo-nkqubo weelwimi wesikolo.
• Iintlobo zenkolo eziza kulandelwa esikolweni.
• Ukuqulunqa umgaqo-siseko wesikolo nenkcazo yomnqophiso.
• Ukuqulunqa imigaqo yokuziphatha kwabafundi ecacisa neenkqubo zoluleko.
• Uhlahlo lwabiwo-mali nolawulo lwemali.
• Ukucebisa ngokuqeshwa kwabasebenzi.
• Ukuxhasa inqununu, ootitshala nabanye abasebenzi.
