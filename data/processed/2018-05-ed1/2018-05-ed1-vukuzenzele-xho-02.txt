Kukho ulwimi olusemthethweni olutsha olu zayo eMzantsi Afrika 

Neo Semono

IKhelobedu ingalulwimi olusemthethweni lwe-12 eMzantsi Afrika ngoku hambelana nokubuyiselwa kwesidima seenkokeli zo mthonyama.
UMongameli Cyril Rama phosa ubhengeze oku nge thuba kubanjwe umsitho wokubeka ngokusesikweni iKumkanikazi yesizwe saBa lobedu eMokwakwaila Stadi um eBolobedu, eGa-Modjadji eLimpopo.
IKhelobedu lulwimi olu thethwa sisizwe saBalobedu apho uKumkanikazi we Mvula wesizwe saBalobedu ongekabekwa, eza kuthatha esi sikhundla xa eneminyaka eli-18, emva kokuba ebekwe ngokusesikweni njengoKu mkanikazi Modjadji we-VII.
Uza kufikelela ebuntwini obudala ngokwamasiko nezi -thethe zesizwe saBalobedu.
“Ukubekwa kwale Ku mkanikazi kuyimbali kwaye uMama Winnie Madikize la-Mandela ebeza kuvuyela kakhulu. Esi sisizwe sokuqa la futhi nekukuphela kwaso esiphethwe yiKumkanikazi kweli lizwe nakweli lizwe kazi liphela,” utshilo uMo ngameli Ramaphosa.
UMongameli Ramaphosa uphinde wambophelela urhu lumente kwiphulo lokubu yisela isidima newonga lee nkokeli zomthonyama.
IKumkanikazi yeSizwe sa Balobedu yavunywa ngoku sesikweni ngenyanga yo Kwindla ngowama-2016  emva kokuba iKomishini engeeMbambano namaBa ngo eeNkokeli zoMthonyama (i-CTLDC) iqhube uphando ngendawo esibekwa kuyo isi zwe saBalobedu, ngokwezice lo ezangeniswa kwi-CTLDC.  “Urhulumente uzimisele ukuqinisekisa ukuba ayafeze kiswa amasolotya eSahluko 12 soMgaqo-siseko, ingaku mbi elokubuyiselwa kwesidi ma seenkokeli zomthonyama kweli lizwe lilawulwa ngo kwentando yesininzi pha ntsi koMgaqo-siseko,” utshi lo uMongameli Ramaphosa.
Uthe ukuvunyelwa uku ba abantu basetyhini babe kwe njengeeKumkanikazi eMzantsi Afrika kubonisa ukuzibophelela kumthetho ohlonipha ukubaluleka ko lingwano phakathi kwabase tyhini namadoda, ngeli thuba ihlonitshwayo indima yoku khokela edlalwa ngooma ma kuwo onke amabakala obomi.
“Iinkokeli zomthonyama zinendima ebalulekileyo ekufuneka ziyidlale ekwa kheni isizwe esingahlu tshwa yindlala, esingena ngxaki yentswelo-ngqesho kunye neyokungalingani.
Iinkokeli zethu zomthonyama kufuneka zibe phambili kwi dabi lokulwela ukubuyise lwa kamalungelo kunye nesi dima sabantu bakuthi.”Uphinde wayibethelela i ndima ebalulekileyo edlalwa zinkokeli zomthonyama ekubuyiseleni umhlaba  kubanikazi bawo abafanele kileyo.
“Kufuneka baqinisekise ukuba umhlaba ubuyiselwa ebantwini abawusebenzayo futhi abo babuyiselwe u mhlaba kufuneka babe nazo izixhobo zokuwusebenza,” utshilo uMongameli Rama phosa.
Abantu abaqikelelwa  kwizigidi ezingama-25 aba hlala emaphandleni bapha ntsi kweenkokeli zomtho nyama.
