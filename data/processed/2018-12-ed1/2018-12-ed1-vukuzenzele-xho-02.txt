Iindlela zokuzigcina ukhuselekile ngezi holide zeKrisimesi

Dale Hes 

Iiholide zeKrisime si zilixesha leziyu nguma nolonwa bo. Kodwa zikwalixesha lokuxhaphaka kwezehlo ezifana neengozi zeemoto, ubusela nolwaphu lo-mthetho. Sebenzisa ezi ngcebiso ukuqinisekisa ukuba ukhuselekileyo futhi uyalonwabela eli xesha nezihlobo zakho.
Hlala ukhuselekile ezindleleniIingozi zendlela zanda kakhulu ngexesha leeholide zeKrisimesi, futhi zibulala abantu abaninzi. Ukuze uhlale ukhuselekile, qinisekisa ukuba:
•	Uyasihlola isithuthi sakho phambi kokuba ungene endleleni ukuya apho usingise khona, ukuqinisekisa ukuba imoto yakho ikumga ngatho ofanelekileyo.
•	Wena nabakhweli ba kho nihlala niwanxibile amabhanti okhuselo.
•	Uyaphumla rhoqo emva kweeyure ezimbini xa uqhuba imigama emide.
•	Nokuba kuyintoni na ungaqhubi xa usele.
•	Qhuba ngesantya  esimiselweyo futhi uthobele yonke eminye imithetho yezendlela.
•	Uyaziqaphela ezinye izithuthi nabahambi ngeenyawo endleleni.
•	Awusebenzisi mnxeba xa uqhuba.
Ukhuseleko lwabantwanaIindawo ezininzi zoku chithela iiholide zizindawo ezixakeke kakhulu, into ethetha ukuba ungala hlekana nomntwana wa kho phakathi kwabantu abaninzi.
Kukwabaluleke kakhulu ukubabeka esweni abantwana bakho xa besenza imidlalwana efana nokuqubha elwandle okanye bedlala kwiindawo zokudlala. Nazi iingcebiso zokugcina abantwana ba kho bekhuselekile:
•	Qinisekisa ukuba abantwana abancinci babaphantsi kweliso lomntu omdala okhathalayo.
•	Emotweni qinisekisa ukuba abantwana abancinci banxityiswe amabhanti.
•	Fundisa abantwana bakho abadala ukuba bayazi iinombolo yomnxeba wakho weselula ukulungiselela xa kunokuvela ingxaki.
•	Qinisekisa ukuba abantwana abanga kwaziyo ukuqubha kakuhle bafakwa amabhanti asenga lweni avuthelwayo xa bequbha.
•	Fundisa abantwana bakho ukuba bangakhe balinge bakhwele kwisithuthi somntu abangamaziyo kwaye bangathethi nabantu abangabaziyo.
•	Mbambe ngesandla umntwana wakho xa ukwiindawo ezixa kekileyo naxa uwela indlela.
•	 Funda uncedo lokuqala lwabaqalayo ukuze ukwazi ukunceda xa kunokuvela ingxaki yezonyango.
Ulwaphulo-mthethoKukho ukwanda kwe mikhuba efana nokuqhe kezwa kwezindlu nobusela ngeli xesha leeholide zeKrisimesi. Hlala ubaze amahle ngawo onke amaxesha uze uthathe la manyathelo okhuselo alandelayo:
•	Xa uhamba usiya kuchitha iholide yakho qinisekisa ukuba iifestile zivaliwe neengca ngo zekhaya lakho zi tshixiwe•	Ukuba omnye waba mmelwane bakho uza kuba ekhona, mcele ukuba abeke iliso kwindlu yakho.
•	Ngeli xesha ukwiholide, zigcine endaweni efihlakeleyo izinto zakho ezibalulekileyo ezifana neminxeba yeselula nezipaji.
•	Yigcine uyitshixile imo to yakho xa uyishiya nokuba lixesha elingakanani na. Zama uku livula icango ngoku sele litshixiwe ukuqinisekisa ukuba litshixiwe ngenene.
•	Zibeke ekuhleni  iinombolo zikaxakeka ezifana neye-ambulensi neyamapolisa.
