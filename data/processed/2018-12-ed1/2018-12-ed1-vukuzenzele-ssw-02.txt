Tindlela longatigcina ngato uphephile kulesikhatsi semaholide Akhisimusi

Dale Hes

Sikhatsi semaholi de Akhisimusi  sikhatsi senjabulo.
Kodvwa siphindze siletse bungoti lobukhulu lobu fana netingoti tetimoto, kweba nalamanye emacala. Landzela leticwayiso kucinisekisa kutsi uba nesikhatsi lesiphephile na lesitfokotisako netihlobo takho.
Hlala uphephile emigwaceniTingoti temgwaco tiba tini ngi kakhulu ngesikhatsi semaholide Akhisimusi, lapho bantfu labanengi bashona. Kute uhlale uphe-phile, cinisekisa kutsi:
•	Wenta luhlolo lwemoto ngembi kwekucala lu-hambo, kute ucinisekise kutsi imoto yakho isesi-meni lesikahle.
•	Wena nebagibeli bakho nihlala nibophe ema bhandi.
•	Utsatsa likhefu njalo emva kwemahora  lamabili uma ushayela libanga lelidze.
•	Gwema kushayela udza kiwe.
•	Landzela silinganiso sesivinini lesibekiwe bese ulandzela yonkhe imitse tfo yemgwaco.
•	Caphela letinye tigitje lwa nalabahamba nge tinyawo.
•	Gwema kusebentisa lucingo uma ushayela.
Kuvikeleka kwebantfwanaTindzawo tetiningi letivakashelwako tihlala timatasatasa, lokusho kutsi kunematfuba ekutsi u mntfwana wakho advuke lapho kugcwele khona bantfu.
Kubalulekile futsi kutsi uhlale ubagadzile bantfwa na bakho uma benta tintfo letifana nekubhukusha e lwandle noma badlala eti ndzaweni tekudlala. Nati letinye ticwayiso tekugci na umntfwana wakho a phephile: •	Cinisekisa kutsi ba ntfwana labancane bahlala bagadziwe ngu-muntfu lomdzala.
•	Emotweni, cinisekisa kutsi bantfwana la-bancane bahlala bafase libhandi.
•	Fundzisa bantfwana labasakhulile inombolo yamakhalekhikhini  wakho labatayishayela uma basesimeni lesibu-cayi.
•	Cinisekisa kutsi ba ntfwana labangakwati kubhukusha kahle ba gcokiswa tinsita teku bhukusha. •	Fundzisa bantfwa-na kutsi bangagibeli imoto yemuntfu la-bangamati futsi banga khulumi nemuntfu la-bangamati.
•	Bamba umntfwana ngesandla uma useti ndzaweni letimatasatasa nanoma weca umgwaco.
•	Fundza tintfo letisise-kelo telusito lwekucala kute ukhone kutise-bentisa uma usesimeni setemphilo lesibucayi.
BugebenguLiyakhula lizinga letehlaka-lo tekugcekeza etindlini kanye netekweba ngesikha tsi semaholide Akhisimusi. Hlala ucaphele ngaso sonkhe  sikhatsi bese ulandzela nati tindlela tekutivikela:
•	Uma uyovakasha, cinisekisa kutsi onkhe emafasitelo netivalo tendlu yakho tikhiyi-we.
•	Uma munye kubo-makhelwane bakho atawubakhona, mcele akubukele indlu yakho.
•	Ngalesikhatsi uvaka shile, cinisekisa kutsi tintfo letibalulekile letinjengabomakhale-khikhini netikhwama temali atikho emehlwe ni ebantfu.
•	Imoto yakho ayihlale ikhiyiwe uma uyishiya ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi. Cinisekisa kutsi ikhiyi-we ngekuhlola tibambo tetivalo temoto.
•	Gcina dvutane tino mbolo tetinsita tesimo lesibucayi letifana ne-tema-ambulensi kanye netemaphoyisa.
