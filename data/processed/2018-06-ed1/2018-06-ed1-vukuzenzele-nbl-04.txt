Ukuvutha Komlilo Kuvula Amathuba Wemisebenzi 

Adele Schormann

IHlelo lokuSebenza ngoM lilo (i-WOF) lilawula amahlelo ahlangeneko wokulawulwa komlilo, lona eliqinisekisa bona kuqatjhwa ilutjha elivela emiphakathini egade idinywe amathuba.  Lisekelwa ngeemali mNyango weBhoduluko, ihlelweli liwela ngaphasi kweHlelo eliNatjisi weko lemiSebenzi yesiTjhaba (i-EPWP).
 Liletha ithemba elutjheni elinengi elingatholi imisebenzi belisebenza kuhle khulu ekukhandeleni nekulawuleni imililo, lehlisa nemithelela emi mbi elethwa mililo yommango.
Ihlelweli liqatjhe abantu abangaphezulu kwee-5 000 abavela emiphakathini etlhagako belibabandule ngelwazi lokulawula imililo, ifundiso ngemililo, uku wukhandela nokuwuphelisa begodu namanye amakghono, afana nesizo lokuthoma, uku kapela, ukupheka, zamaphilo nokuphepha kunye nezokuthintana. Umsebenzi wezokuthintana we-WOF u-Lebogang Mase ko uhlathulule bona ihlelweli liqale khulu ilutjha nokuqatjha abantu abakhubazekileko bona basebenze ema-ofisini wabo asemfundeni. “Sikhuthaza khulu uku thuthukiswa kwamakghono nokunikela ngemisebenzi elutjheni elisanda ukuphuma esikolweni samabanga aphezulu namkha ebasandukuthola iziqu zabo emazikweni wefundo ephezulu. Ehlelweni lokuqa tjha, ama-94% lilutjha, ama31% babantu bengubo kuthi amaphesende amathathu kube babantu abakhubazekileko,” uqinisekise njalo.
U-Lorraine Mokoena one minyaka ema-31 weMpuma langa ungomunye wabaqatjhiweko nozuzileko kilelihle lo. Usebenze ku-WOF iminyaka eli-11 edlulileko.  Ngemva kokuqeda iBanga le-12 waba nobudisi bokuthola umsebenzi iminyaka emibili.
Udadwabo wamtjela ngehlelo le-WOF.
“Ikambiso yokufaka isibawo somsebenzi lo yayibudisi. Isimo somzimba namkha isithombo kuyinto eqakatheke khulu kilomsebenzi begodu abantu abasandukuqatjhwa babekwa ngokuya ngokwesithombo namkha ngokuqina kwa bo. Nawuqeda ukuphumele la ukuhlungwa kufuze godu uphumelele nokuhlolwa zam aphilo bese-ke ngemva kwalokho ukubandulelwa ukucima umli lo kuyathoma,” ubeke watjho.  U-Mokoena uthome uku sebenza njengomcimimlilo osathomako, wasebenza wabe wakhutjhulewa esikhundleni sokuba mndosiphambili omkhu lu wesiqhema, adosa phambili isiqhema sabacimimlilo aba li-18.  “Umsebenzi lo utjhugulule ipilwami. Ngimi ngendwa ose benzako ngakwethu ngondla abentwana bakwethu nabentwa na bami ababili. Ngiyakwazi ukondla umndenami bengibhadele neendleko zesikolo zabentwana bami,” uyatjho u-Mokoena.
Uyavuma bona umkhakha wezokucima umlilo kanengi kumsebenzi wabobaba begodu uthi ufunde bona kufanele aqine, azijamele njengomma kilomkhakha.  U-Mokoena uzilungiselela bona abe mphathi wezemise benzi kilomkhakha eminyakeni emibili ezako. Lokhu kuzome-nza umphathi weenqhema za ma-WOF zoke ezinzinze eMpu malanga.
Njengombana isikhathi semili lo sitjhidele nje, u-Mokoena unikele ngeeyeleliso ezilandelako zokuvikela imililo yemmango:  
• Ungatiki namkha unga kghwari umlilo emmangweni nangabe angekhe ukwazi ukuwulawula.  
• Qiniseka bona unesizo ela neleko kunye neentlabagelo zokujamelana nobunye ubu jamo obugalethwa mlilo.  
• Ungatjhiyi umlilo ungana mtjheji.
• Yelela ubujamo bezulu.
• Ukutikwa namkha ukukghwarwa komlilo emmangweni akukafaneli bona kwenziwe mhlana kutjhisa khulu na mkha kunommoya.
