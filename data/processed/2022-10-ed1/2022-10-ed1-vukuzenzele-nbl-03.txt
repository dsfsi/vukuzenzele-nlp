Umzabalazo Wokubuyisa Ubuntu ESewula Afrika

vukuzenzele unnamed

Mhla ali-12 ku Khukhulamungu ngomnyaka we-1977, u-Steven Bantu Biko wahlongakalela ezandle ni zamapholisa e-Pretoria Central Prison. Wadinywa isithunzi sobuntu, ekumgomo onzinze ekutjhotjhozeleni kwakhe ukuzikhakhazisa kwabanzima ngombalabo.Ngokutjho kwegcwetha lomndeni, u-Sir Sydney Kentridge,  “ukuhlongakala kwakhe kwaba ngokomuntu olahliweko nonganabani arabhalele emedzeni nefluru yamatje ngemaseleni”.Kuhlala kungunoba ngela wamatlhuwo nalokha sekuminyaka kwenzeka, nasicabanga ukuthi uSteve Biko bekaneminyaka ema-30 kwaphela nakahlongakalako. Ipilwakhe yaqinteliswa msinynana lokha nakathoma ukuba sesiqongolweni sayo, ipheliswa ngilabo ababesaba amandla nomthelela wemikhumbulo yakhe mayelana nokuzitjhaphulula kunye nemizamo yakhe yokutjala ukuzikhakhazisa nokuba nesithunzi kibobaba nabomma abanzima.Akhange aphile bekabone lokho abekakubiza  ‘ngonongorwana orhanya zelako’, ukufikelelwa kobuntu bamambala. Nakatlola ngomkhumbu lwakhe, wakhuluma amezwi abanendumela nakathi: “Ngesikhathi esifaneleko sizakuba sebujameni boku thwesa iSewula Afrika isipho esikhulu khulu – ubuso obubonakalako.”Lokha nasithumba ikululeko yethu ngomnyaka we-1994, sazwisisa ukuthi ilungelo lokukhetha laliyi ngcenye yomzabalazo wethu wesithunzi sabantu.Eminyakeni ematjhumi amabili yentando yenengi yethu, sisaqalene neense lele ezifana nomtlhago, ukutlhayela kwemisebenzi nokungalingani ngokomnotho. Ngaleso sizathu vane silibale ibanga esilikhambileko ekuphumeleliseni imigomo esisekelo somthethosisekelo welizwe lekhethu ekungiwo oyisika yemikhumbulo neemfundiso zika-Steve Biko.Ngomnyaka we-1977, umbuso welunya wabulala omunye wabarholi abaveleleko nabathembisako ngelizweni ngokumdima ukugoma, amanzi nokwela tjhwa abekakutlhoga khulu ngemva kokudendebulwa mapholisa wombuso weba ndlululo.Ngemva kweminyaka ema tjhumi amabili, esahlulelweni somnyaka we-1997, iKhotho yomThethosisekelo yathi ukuzaliselela amalungelo asisekelo wazo zoke izakhamuzi nokulwela ukufikelela  amalungelo wezehlalakuhle newomnotho kulitshwayo eliqakathekileko lomphakathi wentando yenengi lokha nakubuyiswa isithunzi esilahlekileko.Namhlanjesi eSewula Afrika, siyaraga nokusebenzela ukuphumelelisa amalungelo asisekelo wamaSewula Afrika woke ukuze baphile ipilo enganamalwele, enganandlala nokudinywa amathuba.Imibuso yentando yenengi esele ibuse amahlandla alandelanako yethule imithethomgomo yokuvuselela isithunzi senengi esalahlekako ngelizweni ngokuletha ifundo, isizo lezepilo ehle, iinkumba nezenzelwa ezisisekelo.NgeSewula Afrika namhla njesi, ifundo enetlha ililunge lo elisisekelo. Umbuso usisa khulu efundweni yabasathomako ekusekeleni imiphumela yefundo yezakhamuzi zethu ezisakhulako ukuze zifikelele imiphumela emihle ngokukghonakalako.Ngesizo lesiKhwama seSizo leeMali kubaFundi  seliZweloke (i-NSFAS)  nezinye iindlela ezihlukileko zesekelo lombuso, iinkulungwana zelutjha lamaSewula Afrika, zibuya emindenini enganabuyo, ziphumelele ukuba bodorhodere, amagcwetha, abotitjhere, abososayensi, bemisebenzi yamakghono nabosomabhizinisi.Urhulumende usekela izakhamuzi ezitlhagako nezinganabuyo ngehlelo leembonelelo zezehlalakuhle nokuvula amathuba wemisebenzi ngamahlelo wombuso aqatjha imiphakathi.KileyaSewula Afrika u-Steve Biko ahlongakalela kiyo, ukukhuluma ngoku tjhaphuluka bekumphambo begodu namajima wezepolotiki bekangunobangela wokubotjhwa namkha oku ngaphezulu kwalokho.ESewula Afrika namhla njesi, abantu abatjha abalingana no-Steve Biko nabangaphasi ngeminyaka  bakhamba phambili ngamajima wokutjhotjhozela abakholelwa kikho begodu bayakghona ukuhloma iinhlangano ngaphandle kokutshwenywa ngemithetho yokuvalwa umlomo. Ikululeko yokukhuluma nokuhlanganyela, ilungelo lokutjhagala nelokulingana phambi komthetho lingelomuntu woke.Njengelizwe, sesikhambe ibanga elide ekufikeleleni isithunzi sobuntu, okumgomo u-Steve Biko egade awu thanda nanyana sisese nebanga elide ekumele silikhambe.Ngaphandle  komsebenzi, ngaphandle kweenkumba, igezi namanzi, ngaphandle kwenarha, ngaphandle kwamakghono namkha amathuba, iingidigidi zamaSewula Afrika zisadinywe ubujamo bepilo eziyifisako nefaneleko. Kungebangelo urhulumende asebenzisana nababambisani ukwakha umnotho ohlanga nyelweko, ukuvula imi sebenzi, ukukghonakalisa amabhizinisi ukuthi aphumelele nokulwa nomtlhago nendlala.Nakutjhejwa ukukhambe lana kwepilo ka-Steve Biko nelifa alitjhiyako, sikhumbula isibawo sakhe esinamandla ebantwini ukuthi babe batlami bekululeko yabo. Lelibizelo lisaqakatheke khulu namhlanjesi njengekanyeni.Kumele sinqophise imikhu mbulo yethu ekuqalaneni nokufikelela izinga lompha kathi otjhaphulukileko nolinganako. Sisoke sidinga ukufaka isandla ngokusebe nzisa amalungelo asisekelo kumThethosisekelo wethu ukwakha iSewula Afrika enganamtlhago nendlala, ukungathuthuki, ubulelesi nenturhu.Njengokusikhuthaza kuka-Steve Biko, asirage ngesibindi nokuzimisela  emnqopheni wethu ofanako wobuntu bamambala.
