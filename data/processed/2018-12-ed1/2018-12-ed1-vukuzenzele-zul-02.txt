Amasu okukugcina uphephile ngale si sikhathi samaholide kaKhisimusi

Dale Hes

Isikhathi samaholide kaKhisimusi isikhathi senjabulo. Kodwa sibuye sandise nezehlakalo zezigemegeme ezifana nezingozi zezimoto, ubusela nobunye ubulelesi. Landela lawa masu ukuze uqikelele ukuthi uphe phile futhi uthokozile ngaso sonke isikhathi, wena nezihlobo zakho.  Phepha emigwaqweniIzingozi zomgwaqo ziba ziningi kakhulu ngesikhathi samaholide kaKhisimusi, zibulale amakhulu abantu. Ukuze uhlale uphephile, qikelela ukuthi:
•	Uhlola imoto yakho ngaphambi kokuba uye lapho uphokophele ukuya khona, ukuze uqikelele ukuthi ise simweni esigculisayo.
•	Wena nabagibeli bakho ngaso sonke isikhathi nifase amabhande emotweni.
•	Njalo ngemuva kwamahora amabili uthathe ikhefu uma ushayela ibanga elide.
•	Ngaso sonke isikhathi gwema uphuzo  ngesikhathi ushayela.
•	Bambelela kwisivinini esibekiwe futhi uqa phele yonke imithetho yomgwaqo.
•	Qaphela ezinye izimoto nabahamba ngezi nyawo.
•	Kugweme ukusebenzi sa umakhalekhukhwini ngesikhathi ushayela.
Ukuphepha kwabantwana .
Izindawo eziningi zamaho lide zimatasatasa kakhulu, okuchaza ukuthi kungenze ka ingane yakho ihlukane nawe esixukwini sabantu.
Kusemqoka futhi ukuqapha abantwana bakho uma kunezinto enizenzayo ezifana nokubhukuda olwandle noma nidlala emabaleni okudlalela. Lawa ngamasu azogcina abantwana bakho bephephile:
•	Qikelela ukuthi aba ntwana abancane baqa shwe umuntu omdala ngaso sonke isikhathi.
•	Emotweni, qikelela ukuthi abantwana abancane bafase amabhande.
•	Fundisa abantwana asebekhulile inombolo yakho kamakha lekhukhwini uma  kwenzeka kuba nesimo esiphuthumayo.
•	Qikelela ukuthi aba ntwana abangakwazi ukubhukuda kahle bagqoke amabhangela afuthwe ngomoya uma bebhukuda.
•	Fundisa abantwana bakho ukuthi bangali nge bangene emotweni yomuntu abangamazi futhi bangakhulumi nabantu abangabazi.
•	Bamba isandla so mntwana wakho uma usendaweni ematasatasa futhi noma weqa umgwaqo.
•	Funda imigomo elula yosizo lokuqala ukuze uyisebenzise uma kunesimo esiphuthumayo. Ubulelesi Kunezehlakalo eziningi zokugqekezwa kwezindlu nezigebengu ngamaholide kaKhisimusi. Qaphela ngaso sonke isikhathi ulande la lawa masu okuphepha:
•	Uma uhamba uya kumaholide, qikelela ukuthi wonke amafasitela nezicabha zendlu yakho kuvaliwe.
•	Uma omunye woma khelwane engeke aha mbe, bacele ukuba baku bhekele indlu yakho.
•	Uma useholidini, gcina izinto ezibalulekile ezifana nomakhalekhukhwini nezikhwa ma	zemali	kufihlekile.	•	Gcina imoto yakho ikhiyiwe uma uyishiya noma ngabe isikhathi esingakanani. Qikelela ngokunyakazisa izibamba zesicabha.
•	Gcina inombolo yosizo oluphuthumayo njenge-ambulensi nenombolo yamaphoyisa iseduze.
