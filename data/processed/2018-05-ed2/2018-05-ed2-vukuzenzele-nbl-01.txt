Kumsebenzi weSewula Afrika ukufundisa ilutjha

More Matshediso

UNgqongqotjhe  wezeFundo ePhake meko nokuBa ndula u-Naledi Pandor uthi iSewula Afrika ingakghona ukunikela ngemifundalize ebentwaneni abathomako emayunivesithi nemaKholi ji weFundo yamaKghono neBandulo leThekhnikha li (ama-TVET) bemindeni edobha phasi naleyo enaba belethi abangarholi kokuya phi emnyakeni lo wee-2018.
UNgqongqotjhe u-Pandor uthe kumSebenzi welizwe ukusekela imindeni etlhagako nedobha phasi.
Begade akhuluma emhla nganweni weembikiindaba lapho aveze khona ikambiso ezokulandelwa nakusatjalali swa ifundo ephakemeko yasi-mahla ngeSewula Afrika.
UNgqongqotjhe u-Pandor umemezele bona urhulumende usise ngokungezelela ngamabhiliyoni ali-R7.166 ukusekela imifundalize ezakunikelwa abentwana abaphuma emindenini edobha phasi nenababelethi abangarholi kokuyaphi abathomako emayunivesithi nema-TVET kilomnyaka.
UNgqongqotjhe u-Pandor uqinisekise bona amabhiliyoni ama-R4.581 abekelwe abafundi beyunivesithi abalungelweko namabhiliyoni ama-R2.585 wabafundi bemakholiji weTVET.
Ngomnyaka wee-2017 urhulumende wamemezela bona ifundo ephakemeko izakuba ngeyasimahla ebafundini abadobha phasi nalabo ababuya emindenini leyo ingenisohla nganyela yayo yaqobe mnyaka izii-R350 000.
Abafundi basekelwa ngaphasi kwehlelo lemifundalize elinatjisiweko, elijamiselela langaphambilini elalihla nganise ukubolekisa nokuni kela ngomfundalize onikelwa siKhwama seliZweloke seSizo leeMali zokuFunda (i-NSFAS).  “Isabiwosisekelo se-NSFAS sokusiza abafundi beyunivesithi nebema-TVET abado bha phasi nebemindeni enga rholi kokuyaphi sizokukhula ukusukela kumabhiliyoni ali-R9.849 ngomnyaka wee2017/18 ukufikela kumabhiliyoni ama-R35.321 ngomnyaka wee-2020/21.
UNgqongqotjhe uthe, “Lokhu kubiza isidingo sokukhuphula izinga lokwenza izinto ngcono kwe-NSFAS.  Singezelele ngeengidi ezili-R105 ngo kweTlhatlha leQhinga leeMali lesiKhathi esiPhakathi komNyaka (i-MTSF) ngomnqopho wokusiza i-NSFAS ukuqinisa ikghono layo lokulawula”.
Abafundi abalungelwe kufumana isizo, eminyakeni yokufunda ngokulandelana kwayo, abatlolisele isitifikedi selizwe loke (samakghono)  emakholiji we-TVET wombuso – bazakufumana ifundo yasimahla ngomfundalize weemali zesikolo neyeentlabagelo zokufunda.
Ukungezelelwa kwesekelo leemali lomnyaka wee-2018/19 kuzakusiza abafundi abazii nkulungwana ezima-458 875 ukobana bafumane imifunda lize.
 Abafundi bemakholiji wa-ma-TVET abalinganiselwa ee50 480 abanelisa iindingo zefuneko yezeenthuthi, yeendawo zokuhlala nokugoma, nabo bazakusekelwa, kanti abazii 82 600 bazakufumana isibonelelo seenthuthi.
UNgqongqotjhe uthe isabelomali esitjhesi silindeleke ukusekela okungasenani  iinkhala ezizii-83 200 kezizii 208 000 (ekuma-40%) zabafu ndi abathomako emayunivesithi ngomnyaka wee-2018.
