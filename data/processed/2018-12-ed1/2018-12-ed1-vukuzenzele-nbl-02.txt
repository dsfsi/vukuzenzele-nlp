Iiyeleliso Ezizakuphephisa Kilamaholideyi KaKresmusi

Dale Hes 

Amaholideyi ka Kresmusi asikha thi sethabo. Ko dwana anjalo nje andisa namathuba wezehlakalo ezinjengeengozi zendlela, ukweba nobunye ubule lesi. Landela iiyeleliso ezi landelako nanzi ukwe nza isiqiniseko sokobana uhlala uphephile bewutha bile nalabo obathandako.
Hlala Uphephile EendleleniIingozi zendlela zanda okurarako ngamaholide yi kaKresmusi, zibulale amakhulukhulu wabantu. Ukuze uhlale uphephile, yenza isiqiniseko sokobana:
•	Ikoloyakho uthoma ngokuyihlola ntanzi ngaphambi kobana uthathe ikhambo,  kobanyana uzokuba nesiqiniseko sokuthi ise bujameni obulungileko; •	Wena nabantu oba khwezileko nihlala ni wabophile amabhande;
  •	Unande uphumula ngemva kwama-awa amabili nayibe ukha mba ibanga elide ngeko loyi; •	Ungathomi utjhayele uselile namkha usele ibe uyatjhayela ngahlanye;
 •	Khamba ngebelo  elivunyelweko kwaphela, nemithetho yendlela uyihloniphe yoke;  •	Tjheja uyelele ezinye iinkoloyi nabakhambi ngeenyawo;
•	Musa ukusebenzisa iselifoni ibe uyatjhayela.

  Ukuphepha Kwabantwana  Iindawo zamaholideyi ezi nengi zinabantu abanengi abazivakatjhelako; lokho-ke kutjho ukuthi umntwana kho angadurha hlangana newoma labantu abazabe balapho.

  Kuqakathekile nokuthi uhlale ubeke ilihlo ebantwa neni bakho nabazithabisa ngezinto ezenziwa lapho, njengalokhuya nabaduda elwandle namkha lokhuya nabadlala eenkundleni zokudlalela. Nanzi ezinye iiyeleliso ongaphephisa ngazo abantwabakho:
•	Yenza isiqiniseko sokobana abantwana aba ncani bahlala beluswe mumuntu omkhulu othembekileko nanyana owazisako;

 •	Ngekoloyini, yenza isiqiniseko sokuthi aba ntwana bawabophile amabhande;

  •	Fundisa abantwana a bakhudlwana inombo ro yakho yeselifoni, wenzelele ukuthi ba kghone ukukufowu nela nayibe kukhona okwenza ukuthi ufuneke ngokurhabekileko;
 •	Yenza isiqiniseko soku thi abantwana abanga kwazi kuhle ukududa bathola iinsiza zokudu-da ezibasiza ukuthi ba ngacwili nabadudako; 
•	Fundisa abantwabakho ukuthi bangathomi bangene ekoloyini yomuntu otjhili nanyana abangamaziko begodu bangathomi bakhulume nabantu abatjhili nanyana abangabaziko.
•	Umntwanakho mba mbe ngesandla na niseendaweni eziphi thizelako nalokhuya naneqa indlela.  •	Funda isizo lokuthoma kobanyana uzokukghona ukusiza nakube ku funeka isizo lokwelapha elirhabako.
UbulelesiBuyanda ubulelesi boku gqekeza emizini nebo kweba ngamaholideyi ka Kresmusi. Hlala utjhejile uyelele ngaso soke isikhathi, uthathe nananzi iiyeleliso zokuphepha: •	Yelela ukuthi woke amafesidere neminya ngo yendlu kukhiyelwe nawukhambako usiya emaholideyini.

  •	Nayibe kukhona uma khelwana ozabe akho na nawungekho wena, mkhombele ukuthi akuphosele ilihlo kwa kho.
 •	Nasele usemaholi deyini, yenza ukuthi ipahla eligugu enjengamaselifoni neempatjhi zemali uhlala uyisithelisile, ingabi semehlweni wabantu. •	Ikoloyakho ayihlale i khiyelwe nawutjhidako hlanu kwayo, nanyana ngabe utjhida isikhathi esincani kangangani. Qinisekisa ukuthi nangambala ukhiyele ngokuhlolisisa iimba mbo zeminyango uku thi zikhiyeleke kuhle na.

•	Beka eduze iinomboro zefoni zabesizo elirha bako abanjengama-a mbulensi namapholisa.
