Amathuba owafumana ebomini, wasebenzise

Amukelani Chauke

ISebe lezoLimo laseNtsho na Koloni liqhuba inkqu bo enceda amafama asa qalayo nalawo sele emile ngenkxaso ukusuka mhla ubani waqalisa ukufama xa efuna uncedo ngezimali ade akwazi ukuzimela.
INkqubo yokuXhasa noku Phuhlisa amaFama incede abantu abatsha abafana  noWayne Mansfield (one minyaka engama-33 ubudala) wasePaarl, osuke ekubeni ngu mthengisi wasesitalatweni osebenzela utatomncinci wa khe eMarikeni yaseKapa xa kuvalwe izikolo waya ekube ni ngusomashishini othume la kumazwe angaphandle iilamuni azilima ngokwakhe kumhlaba awuqeshileyo.
Kwisithuba seminyaka eli shumi edlulileyo, uMansfield engusomashishini osakhasayo oneminyaka engama-23 ubu dala, zange asithathe isithuba somsebenzi awayesifumana kwifama awayethenga kuyo iilamuni awayezithengisa ngelo xesha. Isizathu sakhe yayikukuba ngelo xesha waye ngenamdla kwinto yokuba ngu mfama.
Kodwa ekuhambeni kwemi nyaka, oku kwaye kwatshi ntsha xa ebona indlela iilamu ni ezithengwa ngayo.  "Ndaqaphela ukuba amafa ma nawo enza le nto nathi bathengisi basesitalatweni sasiyenza ayezipakishela iimveliso zawo aze aye kuzi thengisa emarikeni yaseKapa.
Ndaye ndaqonda ukuba thina njengabathengisi basesitala tweni siza kungabi nabantu siza kubathengisela.
Malunga nonyaka wama2013, umfama walengi ngqi wavuma ukuba afundise uMansfield onke amaqhinga okuba ngumfama waza wamni ka umhlaba ozihektare ezili-12 ukuba awuqeshe. Waya wa faka nemibhobho yokunkce nkceshela into leyo yanceda uMansfield watsho wakwazi ukulima.
Ngowama-2015, wakwazi ukuthumela kumazwe anga phandle umthwalo weelamuni ozitoni ezingama-31. Ngonya ka olandelayo, ngowama-2016, umthamo wenyuka waya ku tsho kwiitoni ezili-168.
Oku kwenzeka ngenxa yoku fundiswa nokuqeqeshwa ngu makhwekhwetha kunye  nangoncedo awalifumana kwiNkqubo yokuXhasa noku Phuhlisa amaFama into leyo yamenza wafumana isibone lelo-mali seNkqubo eBanzi yokuXha sa ezoLi mo (i-CASP) ngowama-2015.
Esi sibonelelo-mali samnceda kakhulu watsho wazinza futhi nefama yakhe yakhula.
Ukuxhobisa Amafama NgezakhonoINkqubo yokuXhasa nokuPhuhlisa amaFama ilungiselelwe ukuxhobisa ngezakhono abantu ababehlelelekile ngaphambili, ingakumbi abo baxhamla kwinkqubo karhulumente yokuBuyiswa kwemiHlaba.
Inceda amafama asakhasayo, lawo alimela ukuzondla wona neentsapho zawo, lawo aneefama ezincinci kunye nalawo arhwebayo.Umlawuli weeprojekthi wecandelo lenkxaso yophuhliso lwezolimo kwiSebe lezoLimo laseNtshona Koloni uShaheed Martin uthi le nkqubo incede amafama atsho akwazi uku zimela.
"Sinceda amafama esaqala.
Oku kuthetha ukuba siyawanceda nangenkqubo yokufaka isicelo sesibonelelo-mali aze athi zakuba izicelo zawo zivunyiwe, siwancede aqalise ukulima nokuvelisa," ucacise watsho.
Ucacisile nokuba indlela esetyenziswa lisebe ijonga zonke iimfuno zomfama ize incedise ngeenkonzo zenkxaso ezigcweleyo, eziquka ukuncedisa nangeziseko ezingundoqo.
"Sinazo neengcali ngezoqoqo sho zona ezithi zincede amafama ukuwakhangelisa iindawo anokuthengisa kuzo iimveliso zawo xa sele emile," wongeze watsho.
