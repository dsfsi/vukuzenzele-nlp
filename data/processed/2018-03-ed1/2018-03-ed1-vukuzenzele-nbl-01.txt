Ukutloliswa kwabavowudi kuyokwenzeka ngoNtaka wee-2018

More Matshediso

i-IEC yeSewula Afrika imemezele ipelaveke yokuthoma yokutloli selwa kwabavowudi ama-khetho womnyaka wee-2019. Ikhombele boke abavowudi elizweni loke bona bavaka tjhele iinteyitjhi zokuvowude la bayokuhlola amabizwabo erhelweni labavowudi na-mkha bayokunikela ngemi niningwanabo yamvanje  ukwenzelela ukuvowuda.
IsiPhathiswa esiKhulu seza maKhetho u-Sy Mamabolo uveza bona iintetjhi zoku vowudela ezizii-22 612 zizoku vulwa ngepelaveke yamhlana ali-10 ukuya nakali-11 kuNtaka wee-2018 kusukela nge-iri lobunane ekuseni ukufikela ntambamba nge-iri lesihlanu. Lokho kwenzelelwa bona kube lula kubavowudi ukuqalisisa imininingwanabo nokutlolisa. Nayibe ufudukile endaweni ebewuhlala kiyo nawutlolisa kokuthoma erhelweni labavowudi, njenganje kufanele utlolise ngobutjha esiyingini salapho uyokuvowuda khona.
AmaSewula Afrika aneminyaka eli-18 ukuya phezulu abasele baneNcwadi kaMazisi nofana uMazisi oliKarada bangazi tlolisela ukuvowuda. Angeze wavunyelwa ukuvowuda ngomnyaka wee-2019 nayibe awukatlolisi.
Abazokutlolisela ukuvowuda ngelangelo kufanele baqale bonyana iimphande zabo zilungile erhelweni labavowudi kanti godu banikele ngelwazi elifaneleke ngesikhathi soku tloliswa.
 “Sifuna abavowudi balisebe nzise ithubeli beze ngobunengi babo,” kutjho u-Mamabolo.
“I-IEC bekube gadesi ayinazo iimphande zabavowudi abanga ba ziingidi ezi-2.8. Uhlathulule nokobana kuneemphande eziziingidi ezi-3.5 ezivezwe njengalezo ezingakapheleli. Ubujamobu butlhoga ukuhlazi-ywa okungeneleleko khona kuzakuvela imininingwana ethlayelako le,” Ikomitjhini isebenzise imi thombo ehlukahlukeneko efa ka hlangana ukubuyekezwa kwamarekhodi, amafayela wakade ukuqalisisa nokufaka iimphande zabavowudi abazitlolisa eminyakeni ematjhu mi amabili edlulileko. Ezinye iimphande bazifunyene erhe lweni labavowudi lamakhetho wabomasipala womnyaka wee-2016 begodu nejimeni lokwenza umsebenzi woku tlolisa abavowudi ngokungena umuzi nomuzi ngaphambi kwesikhathi nangepelaveke yokutloliselwa kwabavowudi amakhetho wokukhibelela.
Lapha kukghonakalako, abavowudi abafunyenwe ba tloliswe eeyingini ezingasizo zabo batjhidiselwa kezifane leko ngokwesiGaba12 (1)(d) somThetho wezamaKhetho.
“Semukele besasebenzisa ilwazimininingwana leempha nde  lemithombo yezakhiwo zikarhulumende ezinjengeZiko leemBalobalo zeSewula Afrika (i-StatsSA), i-Ejensi yeeMbonelelo zeSondlo noMhlalaphasi kaRhulumende yeSewula  Afrika, i-Telkom, I-Ofisi yemiSe benzi yePoso yeSewula Afrika nomNyango wezangeKhaya,” kutjho u-Mamabolo.
