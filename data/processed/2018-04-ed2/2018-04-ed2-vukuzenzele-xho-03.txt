I-App ebizwa iGauteng on the move

vukuzenzele unnamed

ISebe lezeNdlela no Thutho laseGauteng  liqalise i– App  yeselula (unxibelelwano olulodwa ngeselula) ebizwa i-Gaut eng on the Move,  lilungisele la inkqubo yalo yezothutho lukawonkewonke ukwenza kube lula kubakhweli uku nxibelelana neendidi ezahlukeneyo zezothutho ngapha ndle kwamagingxigingxi.
I-Gauteng on the Move iza kuphelisa ukungaqiniseki okuhamba nokusebenzisa izithuthi zikawonkewonke, kuba ibonelela abakhweli ngolona lwazi luchanekileyo lwexesha, amaxesha ezothutho namaxa biso eGautrain‚ i-Metrobus‚ A Re Yeng‚ Rea Vaya‚ i-Metrorail‚ iiNkonzo zeBhasi zeGautrain‚ iBhasi yoKhenketho lweSixeko saseRhawutini‚ iiNkonzo ze Bhasi zaseTshwane  neenkonzo zeteksi.
Ethetha ekwaziseni le App (unxibelelwano olulodwa nge selula) kwi-ofisi yomseki wayo eMidrand kutsha nje, uMpha -thiswa wezoThutho kwiphondo laseGauteng (u-MEC) , u-Ismail Vadi, uthe isebe lakhe liyayivuma imfuneko yobuch wepheshe balamaxesha neen kqubo zezothutho ezibhadlileyo njengezibe negalelo kuphuhliso lwenkqubo yezothutho.
“URhulumente wePhondo laseGauteng usebenzise ixesha, amalinge nezixhobo ezifanele kileyo ekuphuhliseni isicwang ciso solu hambo esenza kube lula kubakhweli ukunxibele lana neendidi ezahlukenenyo zezothutho ngaphakathi kweli phondo.
"Sesiwabona amandla  obuchwepheshe bokungeni sa izinto ezintsha eziguqula imo yeenkonzo zezothutho lukawonkewonke. Ayithanda buzeki into yokuba iinguqu zobuchwepheshe ehlabathi ni liphela zivuselele inguqu kwicandelo lezothutho, kwaye kufuneka ezi nguqu sizamkele, siziqhelanise nazo,” utshilo.
UVadi uqinisekise into yokuba eli phondo liza kuthi gqolo lise -benzise ukungena kobuchule obutsha, uphando nophuhliso ukuze kukhuthazwe iphondo elisemgangathweni kuphucul we ukusebenza kakuhle noku fikelela kwiinkonzo zezothutho.
Yakuba ikhutshelwe, le App yenza kube lula kwabo bayise benzisayo ukuba bacwangcise uhambo lwabo ngokukhetha iindlela zezothutho abazi nqwenelayo, amaxesha oku hamba nezinye izinto abazikhe thayo, ezinjengeyona hambo imfutshane nengabizi kakhulu.
 Umfundi waseRhawutini nongumkhweli, uGlen Dlamini, uthe le App iza kwenza ama wakawaka abakhweli azive onelisekile.
“Ngenene yi-App ema ngalisayo le. Lilinge elikhulu lorhulumente kwaye ngoku qinisekileyo liguqula ukwe nzeka kwezinto, ngokukodwa kumntu omtsha ofana nam ohla enyuka esuka eSoweto aye kwiYunivesithi yaseRhawutini e-Auckland Park yonke imihla.
“Noko ngoku ndingazicwa ngcisa ngcono iihambo zam, malunga nezimali zam ndize ndibale namaxesha okuhamba ukuya kufikelela apho ndiya khona.
 “Ndicinga ukuba yi-App emangalisayo, kwaye nje ngomntu ongenguye oweli phondo, iza kundinceda malu nga nokuhambahamba,” utshilo uKhanyo Malunga xa ethetha noVuk’uzenzele e-Blairgowrie.
“Xa ndilapha ndize kudliwano-ndlebe malunga nomsebenzi, ndiza kukwazi ukucwangcisa ngcono, ndize ndingalahleki,” wongeze ngelitshoyo.
Le app ungayifumanela izi xhobo ze-Android ne-iPhone  kwiivenkile ngeevenkile zeApp.
