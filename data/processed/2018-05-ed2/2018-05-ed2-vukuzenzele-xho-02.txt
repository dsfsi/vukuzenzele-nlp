ISebe leMicimbi yezeKhaya lizimisele ukulwa imigca emide

More Matshediso

ISebe leMicimbi yeze Khaya liphumeza  isicwangciso senkqubo sokuqinisekisa ukuba abemi abachithi ixesha elide emigceni.
Ngokutsho koMphathiswa weMicimbi yezeKhaya uMalusi Gigaba imigca emide ibibange lwa bubuninzi obugquthisileyo babantu, indawo enganelanga yabantu abanceda uluntu, imiba yobunkokeli kunye nokuvalwa kwee-ofisi ngeMigqibelo.
Ukulungisa le ngxaki, isebe libala inani lee-ofisi zalo kulo lonke eli lize lijonge ukuba zeziphi na ezifuna ukuba iphu culwe indlela ezisebenza ngayo.
“Siza kuqhuba uvavanyo lokuphanda ngokwaneliseka kwabantu, senze iziko loqhaga mshelwano nemibuzo lisebenze ngokunemagingxi-gingxi, sifu mane isisombululo kwiingxaki yabantu abasuka beze kwii-ofi si zethu bengenzanga dinga kunye nobukhulu bamaziko ethu anceda uluntu, siqwalasele ukuba asinakuqalisa ngenkqubo yamaxesha ahluka-hlukeneyo okuphangela, silungise nobu xhaka-xhaka bethu obu ngazinzanga, sandise izihlandlo ezingabhengezwanga abaphathi abaphezulu abatyelela ngazo kwii-ofisi zethu, siphucule ukuhamba komsebenzi kwaye siqinise ukuqhagamshelana na bantu," uMphathiswa uqinise kisile.
Ezi zicwangciso ziza kuqalisa ukusebenza njengamanyathelo okungenelela exesha elifutshane, eliphakathi kunye nexesha elide.
UMphathiswa uchazile ukuba le miceli-mngeni ayizuku sonjululwa ngamanyathelo okungenelela akhawulezileyo kwaye ucele uluntu ukuba lube nomonde ngelixa isebe liqalisa ngala manyathelo.
“Sizimisele ukwenza konke okusemandleni ethu ukuze si wuphumelele ngandlela zonke lo mlo wemigca emide, nangona kukho imiqobo neminye imice li-mngeni kweli sebe,” uMpha thiswa uGigaba utshilo.
Ngaphambi kwesibhengezo sezicwangciso zenkqubo isebe lenze ingxelo yokuhlola uku fumanisa indlela efanelekileyo yokulawula kakuhle imigca kunye nokuphucula iinkonzo zayo.
Le ngxelo ikhankanye iinkqubo ezingazinzanga, inkqubo yoku ngahambi kakuhle komsebenzikunye nezicwangciso zokuqha  gamshelana ezingahambelaniyo, into leyo ibangela ukuba abantu bangoneliseki nemigca emide.
Ngokutsho koMphathiswa, ezi zinto zilandelayo zenza ibe mbi nangakumbi le ngxaki: 
■ Ulawulo olukumgangatho ophantsi kwezinye ii-ofisi 
■ Ukungasetyenziswa  kwabasebenzi ngokweza khono zabo  
■ Ukungabikho kwenkqubo yokwenza idinga phambi kokuba umntu eze kwii-ofisi zesebe 
■ Ulawulo lwemigca olunge nampumelelo  
■ Ukusebenzisa inkqubo esebe nza ngezandla kunye nezi zisebenzela ngokwazo ngaxeshanye • Ukunqongophala kwee-ofisi zesebe ingakumbi kwiindawo ezinabantu abaninzi • Ingcaciso engeyonyaniso ngokuyekiswa kwencwa di-zazisi eluhlaza  • Izalathisi ezingacacisi kaku hle kwii-ofisi zesebeKukho ii-ofisi zeMicimbi yeze Khaya ezili-184 ezikwaziyo ukuthatha iifoto kunye nezinga ma-411 ezikwaziyo ukunikezela ezinye iinkonzo.
Zezi ofisi zili-184 kuphe la ezikwaziyo ukukhupha nokuqwalasela izicelo zeKha di-sazisi kunye neepasipothi.
Isebe lihlaziya ii-ofisi ezi ngoomahamba-nandlwana ezingama-78 kwaye liqinise kile ukuba ziza kuthunye lwa kuwo wonke uMzantsi  Afrika ngesiqingatha sesibini ngowama-2018.
